# Immersion stops and local host system (Manual Pass 3G)

**Lane:** `RedDirt/campaign-system-manual` · **Markdown only** · **2026-04-28**  
**Public language:** Campaign Companion, Guided Campaign System, Organizing Guide, Field Intelligence, Message Engine, Campaign Operating System, Workbench, Pathway Guide — not “AI.”

**Honesty:** Do not invent confirmed **invitations**, **hosts**, or **RSVPs.** If a stop is not verified, document **unknowns** and run SOP with named owner. **75 counties × ≥3 visits** by November is a **planning target**, not a promise—reconcile with `CampaignEvent`, Google Calendar SOP (`GOOGLE_CALENDAR_AND_EVENT_PIPELINE_OPERATING_SYSTEM.md`), and treasurer/CM capacity.

**Repo:** No dedicated “immersion” Prisma model. Use `CampaignEvent`, `WorkflowIntake`, `CampaignTask`, `RelationalContact` (where policy allows), and off-repo advance notes with PII hygiene.

---

## 1. Executive summary

Two- to three-day **immersion** stops in a town or county maximize **people met** and **ground covered** when a **local host / handler team** runs **forward work** and **calendar** discipline. The **candidate** has **fixed** daily **windows** (breakfast, lunch, after-5/evening; limited workday exceptions). The **event pipeline** must **fill** those windows—not **ad hoc** visibility. This document defines the **immersion model**, **influencer map**, **host team**, **checklists**, **safety**, **Workbench** follow-up, **dashboards**, and **Steve** decisions.

---

## 2. 2–3 day town/county immersion model

- **Intent:** Dense relationship and **sign-up** capture in one geography: **county party** touch, **faith** or **community** (per separate plan), **chamber** / **VFD** / **local** **gathering** when **invited**, **media** hit, **house** **party** or **small** **donor** **circle** as appropriate.  
- **Pairing:** Align with `COUNTY_PARTY_MEETING_TOUR_SYSTEM.md`, `WEEKLY_TRAVEL_AND_EVENT_PROJECTION_SYSTEM.md`, and `FAITH_FIRE_CHAMBER_AND_COMMUNITY_EVENT_OUTREACH_PLAN.md`.  
- **Output each day:** `WorkflowIntake` rows, **opt-in** list growth, **next** **touch** **scheduled**—not **drive-through** only.

---

## 3. Candidate time-window reality

- **Primary windows:** **7:00 a.m.** coffee/breakfast · **lunch** · **after work / evening** (and weekend equivalents when used).  
- **Other workday windows:** Only when the candidate **explicitly** takes time off the **day job**—schedule as **rare,** **CM-approved,** and **compliance**-checked.  
- **No** assumption of **full** **weekday** **availability** for **strolling** or **unstructured** **drops.**

## 7. Local host / handler team model

- **Host captain:** local **point** for **date,** **venue,** **parking,** **intro** **lineup.**
