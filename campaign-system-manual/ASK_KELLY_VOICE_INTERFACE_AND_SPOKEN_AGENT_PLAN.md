## 1. Spoken-answer** **doctrine (when** **audio** **exists** in **a** **future** **build) **

- **Shorter** **sentences,** **fewer** **acronyms;** one **term** in **a** **row,** with **a** **plain** **rephrase** on **ask** (see `USER_FRIENDLY_...` **+** 5C).  
- **Warmer** but **not** **phony;** not **a** **brochure** at **2**× **normal** **speed** in **TTS.  
- **No** **dense** **policy** **dumps;** if **a** **long** **explanation** **exists,** offer **a** **short** **answer** **first,** “**if** you **want** a **bit** more** …**” **(read**-**shaped) **, not **a** **wall** in **a** **phone** **tree** **.**  
- **Answer** first,** **context** second **(same** as **5F** public **UI** **pattern,** **read**-**aloud** **. **)  
- **5D** / **EPI** / **legal** / **fraud** **rhetoric:** same **citations** and **caution** as **text;** **not** a **faster** **paraphrase** that **tightens** the **state**’s **wording** **(see** `CAMPAIGN_COMPANION_ELECTION_QUESTIONS_POLICY` **+** 5D **. **)

## 3. Safety and** **impersonation** **(non**-**negotiable) **

- **Do** **not** **impersonate** **Kelly** in **a** **way** that **confuses** a **voter** **about** **what** is **a** **recording,** a **TTS,** or **a** **human** **callback** **—** **clear** **disclosure** **in** any **IVR/assistant** that **it** is **not** a **1:1** **live** **call** with **the** **candidate** **unless** **it** is **(see** **§**43** **consent) **.  
- **Synthesized** **“**Kelly**”** **audio** = **separate,** **explicit,** **written** **authorization** **(candidate** **+** **counsel** **+** **comms) **+ **a** **consent** **/ **fraud**-**and**-**safety** **SOP,** not **a** **default** in **MVP** **. **  
- **Distinguish** **text** **persona** (Ask Kelly **/ **Guided) **from** a **literal** **voice** **clone;** the **latter** is **a** **higher** **bar** and **a** **different** **risk** **profile** **. **  
- **Sensitive** **topics** (abortion,** **guns,** **contrast,** **press**-**bait) **: **if** a **public** **voice** **line** **exists,** it **should** **route** to **a** **human** **or** **a** **static** **approved** **clip,** not **a** **real**-**time** **synthesis** of **D**-**knowledge** **(see** `ASK_KELLY_...` **) **.

## 5. Future** **phone** / **IVR** **(integration,** not **MVP) **

- **Event** **lookup** **→** public **OIS+**GCal+**5D,** or **TBD,** not **a** **secret** **buffer** of **invented** **RSVPs** **. **  
- **Volunteer** **sign**-**up** **→** public **form,** not **P**-**I**-**I** in **a** **DTMF** **dance** **. **  
- **FAQ** / **canned** **approved** **audio** for **5D+**5F **(short) **+ **deferred** to **static** for **H**-**topics** **. **  
- **Callback** **request** **→** `WorkflowIntake` or **SOP,** not **a** **voter** **row** in **a** **call** to **a** **random** **IVR** **. **  
- **No** **voter** **data** **by** **phone** in **a** **public** **unauthenticated** **line**; **R2+** is **a** **different** **product** and **RACI** **(see** `CAMPAIGN_COMPANION_LIVE_...` **) **. **

## 6. **Accessibility** **(design** **considerations) **

- **Older** **voters,** **low** **literacy,** **rural** **connectivity,** and **disability** **accommodation** are **all** **reasons** **to** keep **a** **simple** **web** and **a** **clear** **human**-**in**-**the**-**loop** path **(see** 5C **+** 5D **) **, not **a** **forced** **voice**-**only** **funnel** **. **  
- If **TTS,** allow **slower** **pace** **and** **repeat**-**key** **(IVR) **, not **a** **single**-**rushed** **script** that **buries** the **5D** **citation** **(cite** in **a** **follow**-**up** **text** **) **. **
