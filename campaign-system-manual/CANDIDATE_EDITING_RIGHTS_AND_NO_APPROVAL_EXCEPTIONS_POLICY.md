# Candidate editing rights and no-approval exceptions policy (Manual Pass 5G)

**Lane:** `RedDirt/campaign-system-manual`  
**Type:** **RACI and governance** — not a waiver of MCE/NDE, LQA, treasurer, counsel, VFR, `playbooks/APPROVAL_AUTHORITY_MATRIX.md`, or `playbooks/ESCALATION_PATHS.md`. This does not grant a public claim that the candidate can instant-publish all edits. See `CANDIDATE_TO_ADMIN_UPDATE_PACKET_SYSTEM.md`, `WEBSITE_EDIT_IMPACT_ANALYSIS_AND_DOWNSTREAM_DEPENDENCY_RULES.md`, 5B, 5C, 5D, 5F.

**Ref:** `ASK_KELLY_CANDIDATE_VOICE_AND_POSITION_SYSTEM.md` · `CANDIDATE_WEBSITE_REVIEW_WIZARD_AND_APPROVAL_WORKFLOW.md` · `MANUAL_INFORMATION_REQUESTS_FOR_STEVE.md` §44

**Product honesty:** A build that routes candidate text to a live public site is **not** claimed shipped in 5G; 5G is manual governance for a **future** flow.

---

## 1. **What the candidate can signal (voice / intent)**

- “This does / does not sound like me” (feeds B-class triage; A-class still MCE+comms when used publicly — 5F A–D).  
- Own biography *wording* preference (B-chasing, not a new unvetted private fact).  
- Softening, clarifying, or removing *values* language (impact tier per `WEBSITE_EDIT_IMPACT_...`: often low/none if no new factual, legal, or $ claim).  
- Flagging a line as **inaccurate** or “not how I would say it” (creates admin packet; may be D in `ASK_KELLY_...` until answered).

**Admin/ops implementation** is still the normal path: **someone** applies the edit in the SOT, runs LQA/publish, and logs provenance (see `CANDIDATE_TO_ADMIN_...` — **not** “candidate clicked = live” on sensitive surfaces).

---

## 2. **No full approval *loop* required when (narrow exceptions)**

When *all* of the following are true for a proposed change (see `WEBSITE_EDIT_IMPACT_ANALYSIS_AND_DOWNSTREAM_DEPENDENCY_RULES.md` — **none** or **low** impact, and packet triage), **and** the change is not a silent rewrite of a 5D-cited or legal line without comms/counsel review:

- No new legal or statutory claim.  
- No new public promise of resources, policy outcome, or office power the SOS does not have (5F scope).  
- No budget, planning, or 4B slider implication.  
- No opponent / contrast or MCE-sensitive line.  
- No new number (5D, MI §41).  
- No targeting, GOTV, or VFR implication.  
- No new external partner, endorsement, or GOP/RSVP date/venue not already OIS-true (5D / Pass 3F field truth).

**Then:** comms/ops may implement with LQA as SOP **without a second explicit candidate re-sign** on that delta — *but* still not without MCE+static path and a human in the SOT (5B, 5D, 5C). 5D lines still need human review if the candidate is “tightening” a cited or legal sentence.

---

## 3. **Approval required when (always trigger full packet + matrix)**

- Legal/statute; election security / fraud; finance/budget/spending; opponent/contrast; new stats; new partner/endorsement; county factual claim; policy outside approved scope; public commitment of resources; paid media; targeting/segmentation; GOTV/voter file (2A, 5B, 5C, 3H, MCE+comms+counsel as applicable).

---

## 4. **What the candidate sees before “final” (design text)**

Plain-language, not internal codes:

- “This would change the **website** only (after the team implements).”  
- “This would also need **Ask Kelly** / knowledge review before it is public in chat.”  
- “This may need **MCE/NDE** and **talking points** alignment.”  
- “This may need **budget or planning** review.”  
- “This needs **comms and/or counsel** before publish.”
