**Public language:** Campaign Companion, Guided Campaign System, Organizing Guide, Workbench — not “AI.”

**Honesty:** **Churches, synagogues, mosques = invitation** **\—** not **staged** **drop-in** without **local** **host** **+** **venue** **rules. ** **Do** **not** **invent** **invites** or **imply** **faith** **leaders** **endorse** **the** **campaign** without **MCE/NDE**-approved fact.

**Steve (context):** **Volunteers** **invite** **Kelly** to **faith,** **VFD,** **chamber,** **fish** **fries,** **chili,** **community** **gatherings**; **pipeline** **fills** **breakfast** / **lunch** / **evening** **windows.**

## 1. Executive summary

A **separate,** **relationship-first** **lane** for **faith,** **service,** and **civic** **host** **driven** **stops,** with **intake,** **promotion,** and **72h** **follow**-**up,** **aligned** with `IMMERSION_STOPS...` and `GOOGLE_CALENDAR...` **(status** from **tentative** to **visited** / **needs** **follow**-**up**).

---

## 2. Faith / community visit doctrine

- **Respect,** **listen** **first,** **no** **monolith** **tropes** (see `FOCUS_CATEGORY...`). **Compliance** and **MCE/NDE** for **solicitation** in **sacred** **/ sensitive** **spaces** when **/ if** an **ask** is **permitted** **locally** **(counsel;** not **a** one-size default).

---

## 3. Churches, synagogues, mosques: invitation-only rule

- **No** **unscheduled** **roaming** of **worship** **space** for **“optics** **only.”** **Invite** with **clarity** of **format** (remarks **vs** **greeting,** time **box).** **Surrogate** **/ volunteer** **presenter** **per** `COUNTY_PARTY_MEETING_TOUR_SYSTEM` **(§14) **+** **MCE.**

---

## 4. Volunteer invitation workflow

- `WorkflowIntake` **(source=faith_invite** **or** **synagogue_... ) **or** form **/ email** **SOP;** **V.C.** and **county** **coordinator** **validate** the **point** of **contact** on **the** **congregation/organization** **side (not** **third** **hand** only).

---

## 5. Volunteer fire department event lane

- **Service** and **rural** **trust**; **not** a **military** **/ uniform** **cosplay;** follow **VFD** **/ county** **protocols,** no **entanglement** with **emergency** **ops.** **MCE/NDE** for **implied** **endorsement** of **a** **public** **body.**

---

## 6. Fish fry / chili / local gathering lane

- **Tie** to `CampaignEvent` **+** `ArkansasFestivalIngest` **(when** used); **parking,** **line,** **sign-up,** **photo** only **as** **allowed.**

---

## 7. Rural chamber of commerce lane

- **Relationship** and **sponsor**-type **leads,** not **D**-only **funnel** **(many** **chambers** are **bipartisan).** **MCE/NDE** for **contrast** and **labor** / **local** business **messaging** **(Pass** 3B/3C style).

---

## 8. Community service / listening posture

- **Ask** = **hear** public **problems,** then **SOS-anchored,** **compliant** **response** (no **unsourced** **opponent** **claims**).

---

## 9. What Kelly may say
