## Relational organizing

- **What** **it** **means:** **Power** **grows** **from** **real** **relationships** **and** **clear** **asks,** **not** **from** **broadcasts** **alone** **. **  
- **Campaign** **practice:** **1:1** **and** **small**-**group** **touches,** **introductions,** **follow**-**ups,** **P5** **habits** **. **  
- **System** **feature:** **Power** **of** **5,** **Pathway,** **onboarding** **ladder,** **thanks** **and** **tasks** **governed** **by** **`WorkflowIntake` ** **. **  
- **What** **not** **to** **do:** **Treat** **people** **as** **rows** **in** **a** **blast** **list** **without** **consent,** **care,** **or** **a** **next** **step** **. **

## Two-step flow and opinion leaders

- **What** **it** **means:** **Many** **voters** **take** **cues** **from** **trusted** **local** **voices** **more** **than** **from** **distant** **messaging** **. **  
- **Campaign** **practice:** **Recruit** **credible** **local** **messengers,** **give** **them** **approved** **language** **and** **a** **clear** **ask** **. **  
- **System** **feature:** **Regional** **and** **county** **roles,** **MCE+** **packets,** **P5** **leaders,** **NDE** **with** **LQA** **. **  
- **What** **not** **to** **do:** **Assume** **one** **national** **message** **lands** **without** **credible** **local** **carriers** **. **

## Diffusion of innovations

- **What** **it** **means:** **Adoption** **spreads** **in** **waves;** **readiness** **varies** **. **  
- **Campaign** **practice:** **Match** **asks** **to** **readiness,** **celebrate** **early** **adopters** **with** **consent,** **lower** **friction** **for** **the** **next** **ring** **. **  
- **System** **feature:** **Progressive** **onboarding,** **role** **unlocks,** **honest** **OIS** **(no** **fake** **“** **hot** **”** **tiles) ** **. **  
- **What** **not** **to** **do:** **Shame** **laggards** **or** **set** **impossible** **first** **tasks** **. **

## Resource mobilization

- **What** **it** **means:** **Campaigns** **win** **by** **mobilizing** **time,** **money,** **attention,** **and** **legitimacy** **. **  
- **Campaign** **practice:** **Clear,** **fair** **asks;** **treasurer**-**governed** **finance;** **recognition** **for** **work** **. **  
- **System** **feature:** **Fundraising** **and** **call**-**time** **plans,** **task** **queues,** **dashboards** **with** **3H** **rules** **(aggregates) **. **  
- **What** **not** **to** **do:** **Run** **unfunded** **or** **unthanked** **pipelines** **or** **opaque** **money** **stories** **where** **disclosure** **is** **required** **. **

## Political process and opportunity structure

- **What** **it** **means:** **Outcomes** **depend** **on** **openings** **in** **the** **environment** **and** **how** **well** **you** **mobilize** **. **  
- **Campaign** **practice:** **Map** **windows,** **build** **durable** **capacity** **before** **and** **after** **any** **one** **headline** **. **  
- **System** **feature:** **Strategy** **manual** **phases,** **4B** **scenarios,** **simulation** **plan,** **weekly** **travel** **and** **event** **projection** **. **  
- **What** **not** **to** **do:** **Confuse** **a** **news** **cycle** **hit** **for** **organization** **. **

## Collective identity

- **What** **it** **means:** **“** **We** **”** **matters;** **shared** **work** **and** **story** **build** **solidarity** **. **  
- **Campaign** **practice:** **Rituals,** **inclusive** **norms,** **rotating** **roles,** **stories** **of** **service** **. **  
- **System** **feature:** **Events,** **thanks** **workflow,** **narrative** **distribution,** **community**-**building** **manual** **(Pass** **5L) **. **  
- **What** **not** **to** **do:** **Default** **to** **clique** **dynamics** **or** **exclusion** **as** **culture** **. **

## Social proof

- **What** **it** **means:** **People** **look** **to** **others** **for** **what** **feels** **normal** **and** **safe** **. **  
- **Campaign** **practice:** **Show** **real** **volunteers** **doing** **real** **work** **with** **consent;** **verifiable** **momentum** **. **  
- **System** **feature:** **OIS** **honest** **visibility,** **testimonials** **with** **LQA,** **public** **pages** **. **  
- **What** **not** **to** **do:** **Invent** **stats,** **crowd** **shots,** **or** **endorsement** **claims** **without** **governance** **. **

## Commitment and consistency

- **What** **it** **means:** **Small,** **public** **or** **logged** **commitments** **tend** **to** **support** **follow**-**through** **. **  
- **Campaign** **practice:** **Micro**-**yeses,** **RSVPs,** **first** **task,** **training** **attestation** **. **  
- **System** **feature:** **Onboarding** **ladder,** **forms,** **event** **RSVP,** **tasks** **. **  
- **What** **not** **to** **do:** **Coercive** **pledges,** **bait**-**and**-**switch,** **or** **shame** **for** **declining** **. **

## Narrative transportation

- **What** **it** **means:** **Stories** **can** **move** **people** **when** **they** **“** **enter** **”** **the** **arc** **. **  
- **Campaign** **practice:** **Clear** **journey,** **service** **ethos,** **voter** **stake,** **bounded** **contrast** **(sourced) **. **  
- **System** **feature:** **MCE** **/ ** **NDE,** **A**–**D** **voice** **discipline,** **Ask** **Kelly** **simple** **view** **rules** **(5F) **. **  
- **What** **not** **to** **do:** **Unsourced** **attack** **lines** **or** **cruel** **caricature,** **forbidden** **by** **policy** **. **

## Agenda-setting and framing

- **What** **it** **means:** **What** **we** **talk** **about** **and** **how** **we** **name** **problems** **shapes** **what** **feels** **solvable** **. **  
- **Campaign** **practice:** **Steady** **cadence,** **values** **first,** **plain** **mechanics;** **local** **proof** **points** **(approved) **. **  
- **System** **feature:** **Comms** **plans,** **Workbench** **queues,** **segmented** **SOP,** **4B** **governed** **sliders** **(not** **auto**-**GOTV) **. **  
- **What** **not** **to** **do:** **Chase** **only** **opponent**-**driven** **frames** **or** **ship** **cynical** **spin** **that** **fails** **LQA** **/ ** **legal** **. **

## Public sphere and deliberative civic culture

- **What** **it** **means:** **Democracy** **needs** **reason**-**giving** **and** **listening** **norms,** **even** **under** **pressure** **. **  
- **Campaign** **practice:** **Town** **halls,** **listening** **tour,** **FAQs** **with** **verified** **sources,** **structured** **feedback** **(5D,** **5H) **. **  
- **System** **feature:** **Suggestion** **box,** **beta**-**to**-**feed,** **trust** **SOP,** **election**-**Q** **policy** **. **  
- **What** **not** **to** **do:** **Reduce** **voters** **to** **props** **or** **enemies** **in** **internal** **or** **public** **scripts** **. **

## Organizational learning

- **What** **it** **means:** **Teams** **improve** **through** **feedback** **loops,** **retros,** **and** **updated** **SOPs** **. **  
- **Campaign** **practice:** **Event** **debriefs,** **no**-**blame** **post**-**mortems,** **documented** **changes** **. **  
- **System** **feature:** **`CONTINUOUS_CAMPAIGN_KNOWLEDGE_INGESTION_AND_REFINEMENT_ENGINE.md` **+ **`WorkflowIntake` **—** **human** **approval** **always** **in** **the** **loop** **. **  
- **What** **not** **to** **do:** **Hide** **failures** **or** **punish** **messengers** **of** **bad** **news** **. **

## Communities of practice

- **What** **it** **means:** **People** **learn** **by** **doing** **together** **with** **shared** **tools** **and** **language** **. **  
- **Campaign** **practice:** **Trainers,** **buddies,** **P5** **pods,** **role** **playbooks** **(Pass** **4) **. **  
- **System** **feature:** **Training** **index,** **Workbench** **brief** **teaching** **lines,** **unlock** **ladders** **(5C) **. **  
- **What** **not** **to** **do:** **Hoard** **knowledge** **in** **ways** **that** **break** **safety,** **succession,** **or** **inclusion** **. **

## Distributed leadership

- **What** **it** **means:** **Leadership** **is** **a** **network** **function,** **not** **only** **a** **title** **. **  
- **Campaign** **practice:** **RACI,** **many** **on**-**ramps,** **mentorship,** **escalation** **before** **crisis** **. **  
- **System** **feature:** **Unlocks,** **regional** **/ ** **county** **roles,** **`ADVANCE_TASK_BUY_IN_AND_ESCALATION_LADDER_SYSTEM.md` **. **  
- **What** **not** **to** **do:** **Chaos** **without** **accountability** **or** **duplicative** **conflicting** **sends** **. **

## Servant leadership

- **What** **it** **means:** **Leaders** **remove** **obstacles** **and** **develop** **others** **,** **not** **hoard** **credit** **. **  
- **Campaign** **practice:** **CM** **clears** **blocks,** **thanks** **with** **consent,** **candidate** **delegates** **well** **. **  
- **System** **feature:** **`THANK_YOU_CARD_AND_APPRECIATION_WORKFLOW.md` **,** **CM** **runbook,** **candidate** **dashboard** **. **  
- **What** **not** **to** **do:** **Disposable**-**labor** **norms** **or** **credit**-**hoarding** **. **

## Movement ecology

- **What** **it** **means:** **Many** **groups** **and** **tactics** **co**-**exist;** **coordination** **without** **control** **is** **an** **art** **. **  
- **Campaign** **practice:** **Allies** **without** **extracting** **their** **base;** **shared** **wins,** **clear** **asks** **. **  
- **System** **feature:** **Endorsement** **program,** **focus** **category** **plans,** **5L** **community**-**building** **doc** **. **  
- **What** **not** **to** **do:** **Trash** **allies** **or** **claim** **support** **without** **consent** **. **
