# Role-based unlock ladders — Manual Pass 5C

**Lane:** `RedDirt/campaign-system-manual`  
**Purpose:** Per-role **Level 0–5** progression: what **unlocks**, what **stays hidden**, which **reports** appear, and which **approvals** apply. **Design** and **SOP** — not a guarantee that every **surface** is **built** (`SYSTEM_READINESS_REPORT.md`).

**Level scale (per role family):**  
- **0** = orientation only  
- **1** = first action  
- **2** = repeatable contributor  
- **3** = trusted operator  
- **4** = lead  
- **5** = strategist / trainer (where applicable)  

**Global gates** that **override** level: `playbooks/APPROVAL_AUTHORITY_MATRIX.md`, `playbooks/ROLE_READINESS_MATRIX.md`, and **MI §40**. Voter row-level access, exports, treasurer finance confirm, public comms, and GOTV / targeting controls are **always** governed by the matrix — not “auto” from level (see `PROGRESSIVE_ONBOARDING_AND_UNLOCK_SYSTEM.md` §7).

## Ladder use in product (when built)

- **Default** a user to their **archetype** + **readiness** — **not** the **max** level their email domain suggests.  
- **“Upgrade deck”** **requests** = **Work**bench **`WorkflowIntake`** or **`CampaignTask`** with **RACI** and **link** to **training** **record**, not self-service admin flags without audit.  
- **Reverts:** serious compliance defect = **downgrade** to **Level 1–2** **with** **clear** **path** back (no humiliation UI).
