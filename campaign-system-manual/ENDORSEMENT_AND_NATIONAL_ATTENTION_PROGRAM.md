# Endorsement program and national attention (Manual Pass 3D)

**Lane:** `RedDirt/campaign-system-manual` · **Markdown only** · **2026-04-27**  
**Public language:** **Campaign Companion**, **Guided Campaign System**, **Organizing Guide**, **Field Intelligence**, **Message Engine**, **Campaign Operating System**, **Workbench**, **Pathway Guide** — not “AI” as a public product name.

**Honesty:** This document defines **categories, workflows, and governance** — **not** specific endorsers. **No** invented quotes, organizations, or endorsement claims. **National** attention and **2028/2030** outcomes are **aspirational** — not guaranteed.

---

## 1. Executive summary

- **Endorsements** are **credibility** and **signal** — they **amplify** a **grassroots**-first campaign when **paired** with **field,** **fundraising,** and **organizing** follow-up. They are **not** a substitute for **relational** work, **county** teams, or **Power of 5**.  
- **National attention** is a **deliberate** **comms** and **field** goal: **earned** **media,** **coalition** **relationships,** and **coherent** **message** **lanes** — always **sourced** and **compliance**-reviewed for **paid** or **regulated** content.  
- **Arkansas Press Association** (APA) **paid** **press** **releases** and **paid** **amplification** (see `PAID_MEDIA_AND_LONG_TERM_INFRASTRUCTURE_PLAN.md`) **extend** **announcements** **only** after **internal** **approval** — **price** **sheet** **pending** (Steve).  
- **Every** endorsement **pipeline** **step** should **connect** to **Workbench** (`WorkflowIntake`, **tasks**) so **nothing** **drops** between **ask** **→** **approve** **→** **announce** **→** **county** **follow**-**up** **→** **volunteer** / **donor** **conversion**.  
- **Repo** **note:** There is **no** dedicated `Endorsement` **Prisma** model in the **documented** **Pass** **3D** **scope** — **process** **first** **(manual** **+** **metadata** **on** **intakes** **/** **tasks** **/** **spreadsheets** **until** **a** **product** **packet** **is** **approved**).

---

## 2. Why endorsements matter

- **Third-party validation** for **voters** **and** **donors** **(when** **real** **and** **appropriate** **to** **disclose).**  
- **Message** **reinforcement** **across** **lanes** **(see** **§3) **—** **aligned** **quotes** **feed** **Message** **Engine** **and** **Narrative** **Distribution**.  
- **National** **attention** **can** **unlock** **coalition** **money** **and** **volunteer** **interest** **—** **only** **if** **follow**-**up** **capacity** **exists** **(V.C.,** **county** **teams,** **house** **party** **hosts).**  
- **Risk:** **Over**-**indexed** **endorsement** **chase** **diverts** **from** **precinct** **and** **county** **work** **—** **balance** **in** **CM** **priorities**.

## 3. Endorsement categories (process — not named entities)

| Lane | Themes (examples — **not** claims) | Typical ask types |
|------|-------------------------------------|-------------------|
| **Direct democracy** | Initiatives, ballot access, citizen voice (policy-bound) | Letter, quote, coalition sign-on |
| **Election integrity / transparency / accountability** | Secure elections, open government, ethics (compliance on all paid) | Statement, panel, joint event |
| **Working-class / worker / citizen protections** | Labor, safety nets, consumer protection (jurisdiction truth) | Quote, co-branded event (if allowed) |
| **Arkansas local** | Unions, educators, business associations, civic orgs, faith communities (**when** **appropriate** and **counseled**) | Local quote, local radio follow-up, county event |

**National** **lane:** National orgs, networks, or figures — **highest** **approval** **bar**; **no** **unsourced** **contrast** **in** **endorsement** **messaging**.

## 4. National attention strategy (directional)

- **Objectives (examples):** **(1) **Credible** **visibility** in **integrity** **/ **pro-democracy** **circles,** **(2) **Reusable** **quotes** for **MCE/NDE,** **(3) **Donor** **and** **volunteer** **pipeline** **—** **not** **vanity** **headlines** **only**.  
- **Tactics (manual,** not **a** **day**-**one** **build):** **Earned** **PR** (relationships, **earned** **media** **outreach) **+ **paid** **PR** **(APA** **or** **other,** **if** **contracted) **+ **compliant** **paid** **social** **boosts** of **approved** **announcements** (see `PAID_MEDIA_AND_LONG_TERM_INFRASTRUCTURE_PLAN.md`) **+ **county** **tour** **/ **stops** **(see** `PRECINCT_PATH_TO_VICTORY_AND_CANVASSING_PLAN.md` **+** **Pass** **3B** **road) **.  
- **Integration:** **Endorsement** **→** **Narrative** **wave** **→** **county** **and** **V.C.** **follow**-**up** **→** **house** **party** **/ **P5** **asks** **→** **fundraising** **(pace** per **$250K** **base) **. ****No** **guarantee** of **“national** **breakout”** in **any** **scenario**.

## 22. Pass 3E — Youth/student quote lane, campus support statements, NAACP and focus relationship lanes

- **Youth / student leader quote lane:** Same **packet** and **compliance** path as other quotes; **no** named students in this repo until approved. **Minors** and **school/campus** settings: follow `YOUTH_CAMPUS_AND_STUDENT_ORGANIZING_PLAN.md` and `MANUAL_INFORMATION_REQUESTS` §21. “Support” language must be **sourced** and may need to be **distinguished** from formal organizational endorsement.  
- **Campus civic endorsements / support statements:** Only when **approved** through this pipeline + **MCE/NDE**; **not** for **invented** org lines or **implied** **chapters** where only **contact** exists.  
- **NAACP / community relationship lane:** **Relationship-first** — `NAACP_AND_COMMUNITY_BRANCH_RELATIONSHIP_PLAN.md`. A **meeting** or **joint** civic education event is **not** an endorsement; **not** public **OIS** “heat” without **governed** **language**.  
- **Focus category statements (EHC, veterans, faith, small business, etc.):** `FOCUS_CATEGORY_ORGANIZING_PLAN.md` — **listening** and **service**; **separate** **alignment** from **formal** endorsement; **EHC** is **not** a **monolith** in messaging.
