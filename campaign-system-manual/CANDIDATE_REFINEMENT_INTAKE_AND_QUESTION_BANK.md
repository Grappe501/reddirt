**Lane:** `RedDirt/campaign-system-manual`  
**Type:** **Strategy** and **governance** **for** a **future** **structured** **candidate** **interview** / **Q&A** / **knowledge** **refinement** **—** **not** a **shipped** **app** or **a** **claim** that **all** **questions** are **pre**-**answered** **in** **a** **database** **today** **.**

**Ref:** `ASK_KELLY_CANDIDATE_VOICE_AND_POSITION_SYSTEM.md` · `CONTINUOUS_CAMPAIGN_KNOWLEDGE_INGESTION_AND_REFINEMENT_ENGINE.md` · `CANDIDATE_WEBSITE_REVIEW_WIZARD_AND_APPROVAL_WORKFLOW.md` · `CANDIDATE_PROGRESSIVE_INTERVIEW_AND_SITE_WALKTHROUGH_PLAN.md` (Pass 5G — **progressive** **Q** from **this** **bank,** not **all** at **once) ** · `playbooks/APPROVAL_AUTHORITY_MATRIX.md` · `playbooks/ESCALATION_PATHS.md` · `CAMPAIGN_COMPANION_ELECTION_QUESTIONS_POLICY.md` (election slice) · `MANUAL_INFORMATION_REQUESTS_FOR_STEVE.md` **§**43 · **§**44

**Column key (tables below):** **Risk** = L (low) / M / H / C (crisis) · **Type** = **S** = SOS**-**core, **V** = values**-**adjacent, **N** = non**-**SOS (still may need an answer) · **Rvw** = comms (c) / counsel (l) / both (b) / — (low**-**risk) · **Public** = A (vetted/Ask Kelly can answer if knowledge exists), B (values bridge from doctrine), D (must defer; internal refinement)

## 1. How the agent **identifies** **gaps**

- **Missing:** **retrieval** or **LQA** **has** no **A/B** **chunk** for **the** **topic,** and **C** is **inadequate** **(too** **specific) **.  
- **Weak:** **two** **approved** **sources** **conflict,** or **a** **public** **question** **exposes** **a** **hole** in **5D** or **MCE** **(numbers,** **legal) **.  
- **Inconsistent:** **site** **vs** **manual** **vs** **last** **public** **quote** **—** **comms** **triage,** not **a** **model** **“**pick** a** **side****.”  
- **All** of **the** **above** **generate** a **refinement** **row** (topic,** **risk,** **source,** no **raw** **unauthorized** PII) **and** follow **`CONTINUOUS_...` **states** **.**

---

## 2. How **live** **questions** **become** **candidate** **prompts**

1. **Intake** **(public,** **volunteer,** **inbox) **: **classify** **topic** **(see** **categories** **below) **+ **urgency,** not **a** **name** **+** **address** in **a** **public**-**facing** **log** for **a** **model.  
2. **Deduplication:** **merge** **similar** **FAQs,** not **N** **separate** **angry** **tickets** **about** **one** **theme** **(operator** **view) **.  
3. **Prioritize** (next **section) **.  
4. **Candidate** **sees** **a** **curated** **list** (not **a** **firehose) **+ **MCE/NDE** for **contrast,** not **a** **chat** **bypass.  
5. **Answer** **/ ****edit** **/ ****reject** with **RACI;** **A**-**class** only **after** **review** per **governance** in **`CONTINUOUS_...` **.**

---

## 3. Prioritization (non**-**exhaustive)

| **Signal** | **Rise** in **queue** when **…** |
|------------|----------------------------------|
| **Frequent** **voter** **asks** | **Same** **tag** in **K** in **7d,** or **OIS/FAQ** **clicks,** or **triage** **(when** **built) ** — **anonymized/aggregated** **.** |
| **High**-**risk** / **C** | **Press,** **contrast,** **legal,** **safety,** **named** person **(counsel) **. |
| **Debate/press** **likelihood** | **MCE+comms** **flag**; **not** a **model** **guess** of **“**this** will **hit** a **debate**”** from **a** **single** **thread** without **ops** **input** **. |
| **Non**-**SOS** **unavoidable** | **H**-**level** in **N**-**row** in **a** **key** **county,** or **GOP/NAACP/union** **listening** **tour** **pattern** (see 5E/3E/3F) **. |
| **Crisis** | **`ESCALATION_PATHS` **+** **owner,** not **a** **chat** **auto**-**response** **.** |

## 4. **Workflow** (public** **→** **knowledge** **base) **

**Question** **asked** by **public** **→** **If** **Level** **A** **(or** **safe** **B** **+** **rules) **: **short** **answer** in **Ask** **Kelly** **(see** public **simple** **view) **. **If** **not: ** public **stays** **civil** + **values** + **CTA,** **and** **internal** **creates** **refinement** **item** **(D) **. **→** **Candidate** **drafts/answers** **(private** **tool) ** **→** **Comms** **(and** **counsel** **if** **needed) ** **→** **Approved** **statement** **enters** **A**-**knowledge,** with **provenance,** not **a** **silent** **model** **update** **.**
