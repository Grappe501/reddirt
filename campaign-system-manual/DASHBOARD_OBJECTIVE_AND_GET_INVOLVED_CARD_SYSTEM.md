## 1. Required card types (design target)

| Card | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| **Today’s** **objective** | **One** **clear** **job** **for** **this** **role** **today** **. ** ** |
| **This** **week’s** **objective** | **Theme** **/ ** **through**-**line** **(comms,** **field,** **training) ** **. ** ** |
| **This** **month’s** **objective** | **Strategic** **shape,** not **a** **fantasy** **stat** **. ** ** |
| **Why** **this** **matters** | **Service** **/ ** **trust** **/ ** **inclusion** **—** **no** **lecture** **. ** ** |
| **Your** **next** **best** **action** | **Single** **primary** **CTA** **(see** **5C) ** **. ** ** |
| **Help** **someone** **else** | **Relational** **organizing** **(P5,** **intro) ** **. ** ** |
| **Ask** **Kelly** **/ ** **explain** **why** | **Plain** **answer** **path** **(5H) ** **. ** ** |
| **Suggest** **an** **improvement** | **Structured** **suggestion** **(5H) ** **. ** ** |
| **Thank** **someone** | **Triggers** **`THANK_YOU_...` ** **;** not **auto**-**send** **sensitive** **(MI) ** **. ** ** |

**Rules:** **One** **clear** **action** **per** **card;** **plain** **English;** **role**-**aware;** **no** **shame;** **no** **clutter;** **progressive** **unlocks;** **mobile** / **iPad**-**friendly** **(5C) **. **

## 2. Examples by role (tangible; no PII)

- **Candidate:** *Review* **[page]** *; sign one thank-you or delegate* **(see** `CANDIDATE_DASHBOARD_...` **).** ** **  
- **CM:** *P0* **sweep;** *one* **approval** *block* **(matrix) **. ** ** **  
- **New** **volunteer:** *Complete* **[onboarding** **step] **; *text* **one** **person* **(P1) **. ** ** **  
- **P5** **member:** *Invite* **one* **reliable* **; *log* **a** *relationship* **action* **(P5) **. ** ** **  
- **P5** **leader:** *Triage* *team* **stuck** **;** *nudge* *one* **stale* **(counts** **not** **names** **in** **public) **. ** ** **  
- **County** **coordinator:** *One* **OIS* **-** *honest* **gap* **+** *one* **event* **/ ** *host* **nudge* **. ** ** **  
- **Comms** **lead:** *LQA* **-** *ready* *slice* *only* **(no** *solo* **ship) **. ** ** **  
- **Data** **steward:** *No* **VFR* **in** *brief;* *access* **in** *VFR* *role* *only* **(RACI) **. ** ** **  
- **Beta** **volunteer:** *One* **suggestion* **+** *one* **“*why*”* **(5H) **. ** ** **
