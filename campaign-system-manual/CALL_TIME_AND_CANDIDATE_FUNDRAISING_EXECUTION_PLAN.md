# Call time and candidate fundraising execution plan (Manual Pass 3G)

**Lane:** `RedDirt/campaign-system-manual` · **Markdown only** · **2026-04-28**  
**Public language:** Campaign Companion, Workbench, Pathway Guide — not “AI.”

**Honesty:** **Do** **not** **publish** **donor** **tiers,** **amounts,** or **“likely** **$X”** **in** **this** **repo** **(no** **PII,** no **invented** **pledges).** All **asks** are **MCE/NDE**-fit where public.

**Steve (context):** **Structured** **call** time; **drive**-**time** **calls** where **safe;** **one** **after-****work** **evening** **/ week** for **dialing.** `MANUAL_INFORMATION_REQUESTS` §**28** expands **availability.**

---

## 1. Executive summary

**Call** **time** is a **governed** **pipeline:** **lists** (donor, **host,** **county** **party,** **warm** **leads) **+** **scripts** (framework only here) + **outcomes** **in** **Workbench** + **72h** **next** **steps** **(schedule** or **abandon** **with** **log).**

---

## 2. Candidate time constraints

- **Tied** to **L&D** **day** **job** and **public** **events**; **not** an **8-hour** **call** **day** **on** **weeknights** by default.  
- **CM** **approves** **call** **blocks** in **the** **single** **calendar** **SOP** (`GOOGLE_CALENDAR...`).

---

## 3. Drive-time call model

- **When:** **Solo** **driving,** **hands-free,** **where** **permitted;** **no** **calls** in **jurisdictions** **/ conditions** that **are** **unsafe** **or** **illegal.** **Stop** **if** **cognitive** **load** **(weather,** **routing).**  
- **List:** **Short** **(5** **+/-** **names),** **pre-scored,** **with** **one-line** **context** (last gift, last event) **on** **paper** **or** **operator** **view** only—**not** in **this** **repo.**

---

## 4. One after-work call-time evening per week

- **Recurring** **block** in **Google** + internal **(e.g. “Call** **time** **Wed** **6:30** **+/-”)** **\—** **CM** and **treasurer** **own** the **slot** **\—** `MANUAL_INFORMATION_REQUESTS` **§**28.

---

## 5. Structured call list priority model (conceptual)

1) **Urgent** **/ time-bound** (deadline) **2)** **Major** **/ max** **ask** (policy) **3)** **House** **party** **hosts** **in** **ramp** **4)** **County** **party** **/ warm** **D** from **intake** **5)** **Thank-you** **/ stewardship** **6)** **Reactivation** **(cold,** deprioritize).

---

## 6. Donor tiers (operational, not in repo)

- **Internal** **A/B/C** **+** **prospect;** **treasurer** **names** **thresholds;** not **a** public **brag** **in** **this** **manual.**

---

## 7. Warm vs cold calls

- **Warm** = **known** **relationship** **/ prior** **gift** **/ event;** **higher** **connect** **rate,** **shorter** **ask.** **Cold** = **rare** for **the** **candidate;** **delegate** to **finance** **team** or **P2G** when **possible.**

---

## 8. County party contact calls

- **After** list **SOP** in `CONTACT_LIST_INTAKE...`; **MCE/NDE** on **solicitation** and **neutrality** if **in** public context.

---

## 9. House party host calls

- **Tie** to `FUNDRAISING_AND_VOLUNTEER_ACCELERATION_PLAN.md` and **host** **pipeline;** **log** in **tasks.**

---

## 10. Major donor calls

- **Treasurer** / **owner** may **be** on **line;** **one** **clear** **ask,** **documented** **outcome,** no **leaked** **details** in **chat** **/ repo.**

---

## 11. Thank-you calls

- **Stewardship** tranche; **affects** **retention;** still **outcome**-**logged** (receipt **linked** in **operator** system when **policies** **allow**).

---

## 12. Follow-up cadence

- **Pledge** not **captured** = **24–48h** **staff** follow-up. **“Maybe” =** next **toucher** in **2** **weeks** (example—**tune** with **CM).**

---

## 13. Call script framework (non-binding template)

- **Greeting,** **permission** **(time),** **one** **sentence** **why,** **listen** **30–60s,** **ask,** **confirm** **next** **step,** **thank** **\—** all **MCE/NDE**-approved for **solicitation.**

---

## 14. Call outcome logging

- **Code** **e.g. connected / voicemail / no / ask_yes_pledge / ask_soft / callback / do_not_call** **\—** in **private** **fields** or **tasks,** not **public** **export.**

---

## 15. Workbench task flow

- **Spawn** from **triage** of **dialer** list **\—** `CampaignTask` **+** `WorkflowIntake` **(when** used) **+** `RelationalContact` **touch** (policy).

---

## 16. Dashboard requirements

- **Calls** **attempted,** **connect** **rate,** **$** **pledged/secured** (treasurer), **stewardship** **lag,** no **per-donor** **names** in **aggregate** **KPIs.**

---

## 17. Risks and guardrails

- **Burnout,** **compliance,** **wrong** **ask** in **context,** **PII** **leak** **\—** **counsel** **/ treasurer** **gates.**

---

## 18. Steve decision list

- **Exact** **one** **evening** **/ week** **slot,** **target** **dials,** and **$** **goal** for **base** **pace** **(not** a **public** **repo** number).  
- **Whether** **drive-time** is **credited** in **KPIs** and **how.**  
- **List** **priority** when **tour** **/ immersion** **conflicts** with **dialer** time.
