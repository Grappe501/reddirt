## 1. Executive summary

- **Paid media is an amplifier, not the strategy.** The strategy is **community teams**, **county** **infrastructure**, **house** **parties**, **road** **stops**, **volunteer** **pathways** (Pathway Guide), and **honest** Field Intelligence — all orchestrated through **Workbench** and **human** approval for **regulated** or **contrast** content.  
- **Grassroots infrastructure is the strategy.** Every program should increase **durable** capacity: people who **show** **up**, **follow** **up**, and **stay** after 2026.  
- **Arkansas Press Association (APA)** is intended as a **vendor** / **channel** **partner** for **bundled** access to **social** **buys**, **digital** **buys**, **local** **radio**, **local** **TV** (if budget allows), **local** **paper** **ads**, and **paid** **press** **releases** for **major** **events** and **announcements** — **subject** to **contract**, **price** **sheet** (pending), and **compliance**.  
- **Every paid dollar** should **intend** to drive **at** **least** **one** of: **recruitment** (volunteer / host / P5), **fundraising** (compliance-first), **message** **reinforcement** (MCE/NDE-aligned), **event** **attendance**, or **county** **team** **formation** — with a **written** **purpose**, **CTA**, **geography**, and **approval** **owner** before **spend**.  
- **The 2026 campaign is also infrastructure for 2028 and 2030.** Data, relationships, and **trained** **leaders** should **outlive** **one** **race** when **governed** by **consent**, **ethics**, and **compliance** — see **§9–10**.

## 4. Social media paid ramp

- **Small** **test** **buys** **first** **(banded** **budgets,** **not** **one** **big** **blast) **—** **learn** **CTR** **and** **signup** **quality** **before** **scale**.  
- **Message** **testing:** **A/B** **only** **within** **approved** **MCE** **frames;** **no** **opponent** **claims** **without** **sourcing** **+** **counsel** **as** **required**.  
- **County**-**specific** **creative** **where** **budget** **allows** **+** **NDE** **has** **county** **packet** **grounding**.  
- **Boost** **event** **posts** **/** **house** **party** **invites** **(compliance** **on** **paid** **political** **content**).  
- **Volunteer** **recruitment** **ads,** **host** **recruitment,** **P5** **recruitment** **—** **each** **with** **distinct** **landing** **path** **and** **tag** **(§8).**  
- **Fundraising** **asks** **(compliance** **disclaimers,** **treasurer** **rules) **.  
- **Retargeting** **only** **where** **legally** **and** **technically** **permitted** **and** **under** **written** **policy** **(see** **§6) **—** **do** **not** **assume** **retargeting** **is** **available** **or** **authorized**.  
- **Conversion** **tracking:** **UTM** **/ **source** **tags** **on** **all** **paid** **links** **→** **forms** **→** **`WorkflowIntake.metadata` **or** **equivalent** **(operator** **SOP) **.  
- **Weekly** **optimization** **rhythm:** **Finance** **+** **comms** **review** **spend** **vs** **KPI;** **pause** **underperformers;** **do** **not** **auto**-**optimize** **contrast** **or** **regulated** **copy** **without** **human** **approval**.  
- **Before** **spend:** **approved** **creative,** **budget** **cap,** **duration,** **audience** **description** **(aggregate),** **compliance** **check** **for** **paid** **political** **content,** **finance** **authorization**.

## 5. Local radio, paper, TV, and paid press releases

- **Radio:** **Reminders** **(time,** **place,** **vote** **info),** **event** **promo,** **host** **recruitment;** **pair** **with** **local** **volunteer** **callback** **within** **48h**.  
- **Paper** **ads:** **County** **credibility** **+** **QR** **/ **URL** **to** **form;** **county** **coordinator** **runs** **follow**‑**up** **list** **from** **inbound** **(not** **“**ad** **only**”) **.  
- **Paid** **press** **releases:** **Major** **endorsements,** **tour** **milestones,** **filing** **events** **—** **must** **tie** **to** **NDE** **wave** **and** **field** **debrief** **(local** **story** **queue).**  
- **Local** **TV:** **Threshold** **=** **budget** **+** **creative** **bandwidth** **+** **compliance** **cleared;** **otherwise** **skip** **rather** **than** **thin** **spots**.  
- **When** **not** **to** **spend:** **No** **clear** **CTA,** **no** **follow**‑**up** **owner,** **COH** **below** **floor,** **stretch** **not** **unlocked** **for** **stretch**-**level** **buys,** **or** **legal** **uncertainty** **on** **disclaimers**.  
- **Traditional** **buys** **feed** **field** **by** **design:** **every** **county** **ad** **creates** **a** **Workbench** **queue** **of** **responders** **for** **that** **county** **(§8).**

## 8. Paid media to Workbench workflow

**Target** **path** **(SOP;** **product** **tags** **may** **evolve) **:**

`Paid buy` **→** **landing** **page** **/ **form** **with** **UTM** **+** **source** **=** **paid_{channel}_{county?} `** → **`POST /api/forms` **→** **`WorkflowIntake` **(`metadata` **carries** **source) **→** **`open-work` **/ **Workbench** **→** **assignment** **(V.C.,** **county,** **fundraising) **→** **follow**‑**up** **tasks** **→** **dashboard** **/ **ladder** **update**.**

- **Tagging:** **Every** **paid** **lead** **must** **carry** **non**-**secret** **source** **labels** **so** **operators** **know** **“**cold** **paid**”** **vs** **“**warm** **relational**”** **(follow**‑**up** **differs).**  
- **V.C.:** **Paid** **signups** **get** **structured** **welcome** **+** **higher** **touch** **to** **first** **meaningful** **action** **(or** **honest** **opt** **down) **—** **not** **same** **as** **friend**-**referred** **joins** **without** **thought** **(see** **strategy** **manual** **§7).**  
- **Message** **Engine:** **Suggested** **follow**‑**up** **language** **may** **vary** **by** **acquisition** **source** **(approved** **templates) **—** **not** **auto**-**generated** **contrast** **or** **regulated** **claims**.  
- **County** **coordinators:** **Receive** **local** **leads** **from** **geo**-**tagged** **paid** **routes** **in** **Workbench** **(or** **export) **—** **within** **PII** **policy**.  
- **Power** **of** **5:** **Paid** **→** **signup** **→** **V.C.** **→** **P1/P2/P5** **Pathway** **—** **same** **human** **coaching** **as** **organic** **(no** **second**-**class** **volunteers,** **but** **expect** **different** **conversion** **rates).**

- **Local** **civic** **infrastructure** **is** **the** **product** **of** **this** **campaign** **—** **not** **only** **one** **candidate’s** **win** **metric.** **Field** **Intelligence** **and** **county** **pages** **should** **tell** **true** **aggregate** **stories,** **not** **dox** **voters**.

## 12. Paid media + grassroots integration (flywheel)

1. **Paid** **ad** **recruits** **attention** **(aggregate** **reach) **.  
2. **Volunteer** **follow**‑**up** **converts** **attention** **into** **meaningful** **action** **(Workbench,** **not** **ghost** **signups) **.  
3. **Power** **of** **5** **turns** **action** **into** **network** **(relational** **depth) **.  
4. **County** **team** **turns** **network** **into** **infrastructure** **(OIS** **+** **ladder) **.  
5. **Leadership** **development** **turns** **infrastructure** **into** **future** **candidates** **(opt** **in,** **training) **.  
6. **Future** **candidates** **(2028/2030) ** **turn** **infrastructure** **into** **more** **contested** **races** **—** **directional,** **long** **horizon,** **not** **guaranteed** **outcomes**.
