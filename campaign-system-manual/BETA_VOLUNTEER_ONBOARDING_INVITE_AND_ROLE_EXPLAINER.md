## Main email body (MCE + owner approve list and copy)

Hello,

You’re **invited to help test the first Ask Kelly and Campaign Companion experience** on the public site. This is **not** a finished product for everyone yet — you’re here because we trust you to be **clear-eyed, kind, and specific**.

**What the beta is**

- A **first look** at **guided help** and **suggestion paths** for voters and volunteers — in **plain language**, without technical overload.  
- A chance for us to learn **where people get lost** and **how** **to** **explain** our **organizing** **and** **service** **choices** **honestly**.

**What we’re asking you to do**

- **Use** Ask Kelly **naturally** — as you would if you were a curious Arkansan or a new volunteer.  
- **Note** where the language is **confusing** or a **CTA** **feels** **heavy** **. **  
- **Ask** “**Why is this built this way?**” when something feels odd — the experience is **designed** to **answer in plain** **language** (strategy, **trust,** **accessibility,** **Power** of **5,** **county-** **first** **organizing** **—** not **jargon) **. **  
- **Send suggestions through Ask Kelly** the way we set up, **not** as scattered one-off messages — so we can **triage,** **dedupe,** and **act** with **governance** **. **

**What happens to your feedback**

- Suggestions and questions **go into a review path** the **admin** **and,** where needed, **the candidate and comms** use — **not** a public back-and-forth.  
- **We won’t** **promise** to **do** **everything** you suggest. **We will** use **serious,** **specific** **input** to **improve** **. **

**Ways to help (pick what fits)**

- **Read** the pages the campaign wants eyes on.  
- **Ask questions** and **test clarity**.  
- **Flag** confusing or cold language.  
- **Suggest** improvements **(specific page + what + why) **. **  
- **Point out** missing information where you’d need it as a voter or volunteer.  
- **Say** if **onboarding** **feels** **easy** **or** **not** **. **

**What we need you *not* to do**

- **Do not** **paste** or **test** with **voter** **data,** **donor** **data,** or **sensitive** **personal** **information** **—** use **fictive** or **obviously** **fake** **examples** **. **  
- **Do not** **assume** **every** line is **final** **law**-**checked** or **MCE**-**approved** in beta.  
- **Do not** **share** **private** **beta** **links** or **access** **publicly** **without** **approval** (see MI §45).

**Tone we’re after**

- **Welcoming,** **mission**-**driven,** **clear** — **this** is **a** **service** **SOS** **frame** **,** not **a** **tech** **flex** **. **

Thank you for helping us **earn trust the right way** — one honest line at a time.  
[Sign-off / MCE as approved]
