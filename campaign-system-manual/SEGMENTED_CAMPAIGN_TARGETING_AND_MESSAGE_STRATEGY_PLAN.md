## 1. Executive summary

Segmentation means **planning** **how** the campaign **talks** and **organizes** **differently** by **geography** and **audience lane** using **aggregate, lawful, consent-aware** methods — **recruit**, **persuade**, and **GOTV** — **without** inferring **protected** characteristics, **without** row-level voter detail in volunteer tools, and **without** **public** claims from unreviewed data. The **Message Engine** supplies **drafts** and **checks**; **Narrative Distribution** ships **approved** waves; **paid** and **file** audiences require **explicit** approval chains.

---

## 2. Ethical segmentation doctrine

- **Lawful purpose:** Register voters, persuade where appropriate, turn out supporters — **never** suppression, intimidation, or deception.  
- **Aggregate first:** Geography, **modeled** party/turnout **only** where **licensed** and **counseled** — no **DIY** propensity for public volunteers.  
- **Honest uncertainty:** Where **precinct** data is **missing**, **degrade** to **county** or **region** plans (see `PRECINCT_PATH_TO_VICTORY...`).  
- **Minors and schools:** youth **rules** and **parent** consent — `YOUTH_CAMPUS...` and counsel.  
- **Faith and worship spaces:** invitation-only; no **exploitation** of religious settings for **covert** **data** capture.

---

## 3. Geography-first segmentation

- **Default** lens: **where** we organize and **where** we spend **time** and **money** — **OIS**, **county pages**, **travel** scores, **event** density.  
- **Outputs:** **task** focus and **message** **tones** per **region**, not **secret** scores in public.

---

## 4. County segmentation

- **Variables (examples, aggregate):** coordinator **bench**, meeting **stage**, rural **weight**, fundraising **history** (internal), **NAACP** / **party** relationship **stage** (map-first).  
- **Use:** county **message** emphasis; **travel** priority; **endorsement** cadence — all **MCE/NDE**-gated for public text.

---

## 5. City / community segmentation

- **Where** **precinct** keys **exist**, sub-county planning can use **city** or **community** labels as **organizing** units — **not** as **surveillance** labels.  
- **Comms** avoid **stereotype** copy; **listening** posture in **mixed** communities.

---

## 6. Precinct segmentation when data exists

- **Turf**, **walk lists**, **canvass** shift design — **staff** and **trained** roles only; **lists** stay off **public** surfaces.  
- **Readiness** uses **completion** of **assigned** turf, not public “propensity.”

---

## 7. County-first fallback when precinct data is missing

- **Do** **not** pretend **precinct** **precision**. **Roll** **GOTV** and **field** plans to **county** **capacity** and **honest** **OIS** until data **acquisition** completes — per `PRECINCT_...` and `MANUAL_INFORMATION` §20.

---

## 8. Audience and message lanes (non-exclusive)

- Lanes are **planning** buckets for **tone** and **program** — **not** automatic **profiling** of individuals in public UI.  
- Each lane has a **primary** **owner** (comms/NDE/field) and **compliance** touchpoint when **paid** or **file**-derived.

---

## 9. Recruit / persuade / GOTV framework

| Lane | Goal (plain) | Typical programs (examples) | Guardrails |
|------|--------------|----------------------------|------------|
| **Recruit** | Add **supporters** and **volunteers** | events, P5, campus, county party, house parties | honest asks; no coercion; **opt**-in |
| **Persuade** | **Undecided** / **soft** audiences with **truth**-checked copy | digital, mail, door (where policy allows) | **no** protected-trait targeting; **counsel** on contrast |
| **GOTV** | **Turn out** known or modeled supporters | text, calls, rides (where legal) | **opt**-in; **compliance** scripts; **no** robo-**law** **violations** |

**Note:** “**Soft** Republican / **independent**” and “**non**voter” programs (§17–18) are **high**-**touch** **compliance** — not **guerrilla** outreach.

---

## 10. Socioeconomic and political-context analysis — aggregate only

- **Use** **public** and **licensed** **aggregate** stats (unemployment, coverage maps, **turnout** history) to **inform** **tone** and **priority** — **never** to **label** **individuals** in volunteer tools.  
- **Analyst** outputs go to **CM/owner** as **briefs**, not **public** **dashboards** with **micro**-**stats**.

---

## 11. Rural message lane

- **Emphasis:** service, **infrastructure**, **local** **respect**, **small-town** **economy** — **avoid** **urban** **condescension** and **token** **photo** **ops** (see county party + rural manuals).  
- **Field** pairs with **road** and **county** party **stewards**.

---

## 12. Campus / youth message lane

- **Youth** safety, **parent** and **school** **rules**; **no** **minors’** PII in **any** **public** artifact.  
- **Activation** **ladder** honest — **no** **“every** **campus** **active**” **claim** without **field** **truth**.

---

## 13. 60+ postcard / name-recognition lane

- **Large** **type**, **clear** **asks**, **trusted** **messengers** where available — **postcard** **match** **rate** is **internal** only; **suppression** of **small**-**N** **public** claims.  
- **Coordination** with **NDE** + **print** vendor and **treasurer** **tranches**.

---

## 14. Working-class / family economy lane

- **Themes:** **prices**, **health** **care** **access**, **jobs**, **child** **care** — **policy**-**grounded**, **sourced** — **not** **stereotype** by **any** **protected** **class**.  
- **Paid** and **organic** both need **MCE/NDE** where **public**.

---

## 15. Direct democracy / transparency / accountability lane

- **SOS** office **fit:** **election** **security**, **transparency**, **access** — **no** **false** **precision** about **fraud** **rates**; **no** **undermining** **trust** in **elections**.  
- **Listening** tour for **ballot** items — **neutral** facilitation per 3G listening doc.

---

## 16. County party / rural Democratic infrastructure lane

- **Relationship** **stages**, **not** **transactional** **extracts** of **local** **party** **identity**.  
- **Meetings** **not** **endorsements** — **no** **fake** **chairs** or **dates**.

---

## 17. Soft Republican / independent outreach guardrails

- **Only** **lawful** **contact** methods; **no** **deception** about who we are; **contrast** **only** with **counsel**-**cleared** **language**.  
- **Programs** are **small**, **supervised**, **measured** at **aggregate** — **not** **covert** **persuasion** **experiments** on **protected** traits.

---

## 18. Nonvoter / slow-voter outreach guardrails

- **Registration** **help** and **information** — **not** **pressure**; **no** **misleading** deadlines.  
- **Where** **models** exist, **internal** only; **public** copy stays **values**-**first**.

---

## 19. Message Engine integration

- **Inputs:** **approved** **facts**, **counsel** flags, **tone** **guide** from `MANUAL_INFORMATION` and MCE plans.  
- **Outputs:** **drafts** for **human** **edit** — **not** **auto**-**send** from **simulation** sliders.

---

## 20. Narrative Distribution integration

- **Waves** tagged by **lane** (recruit/persuade/GOTV) and **geography** **band** — **telemetry** on **what** **shipped**, not **covert** **experiments**.
