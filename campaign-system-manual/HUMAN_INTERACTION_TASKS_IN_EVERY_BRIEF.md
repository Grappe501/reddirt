**Ref:** `DAILY_APPROVAL_LAUNCH_AND_TASK_ROUTING_SYSTEM.md` · `THANK_YOU_CARD_AND_APPRECIATION_WORKFLOW.md` · `ASK_KELLY_BETA_FEEDBACK_TO_APPROVAL_FEED_WORKFLOW.md` · `ADVANCE_TASK_BUY_IN_AND_ESCALATION_LADDER_SYSTEM.md` · `VOLUNTEER_RETENTION_ENGAGEMENT_AND_LOW_TURNOVER_SYSTEM.md` (Pass 5K) · `MANUAL_INFORMATION_REQUESTS_FOR_STEVE.md` (§47, §48)

## 1. Required “Human follow-up” section

Every **brief** **template** **(see** `WORKBENCH_DAILY_BRIEF_TEMPLATE_...` **) ** must **include** **a** **dedicated** **“** **Human** **Follow**-**up** **”** **section** with **at** **least** **one** **concrete,** **safe** **action** **unless** **no** **role**-**appropriate** **action** **exists** **(see** **§**4** **).** **

**In** **general** **briefs** **(non**-**R2+),** **use** **role** **/ ** **relationship** **labels,** not **voter** **/ ** **donor** **names** or **row** **detail** **. ** **

---

## 2. Human task types (examples)

- Phone call  
- Personal text (opt-in, governed)  
- Thank-you note or card (see `THANK_YOU_...` **) ** **  
- Volunteer check-in  
- Donor stewardship touch (R2+ / treasurer path as governed)  
- Candidate personal note (voice; **MCE+** for sensitive outbound)  
- County leader call  
- Event host follow-up  
- Beta volunteer response (suggestion path — **5H** **) ** **  
- Suggestion follow-up (Ask Kelly queue — **internal) ** **  
- GOP / civic / community **listening** follow-up **(5D/5E** **tone) ** **  
- “Ask one person to bring one person” (Power of 5 — relationship, not a **dox**)
- **Retention / buy-in check-in** (Pass 5K): “Are the right people not overloaded?” — V.C. + CM-facing, not a shame callout, not VFR (see `VOLUNTEER_RETENTION_ENGAGEMENT_AND_LOW_TURNOVER_SYSTEM.md` + `ADVANCE_TASK_BUY_IN_AND_ESCALATION_LADDER_SYSTEM.md`).

| Field | Notes |
|-------|--------|

| **Due** **date** **/ ** **window** | **or** TBD with **parking** **rule** **. ** ** |

## 4. Rule: at least one safe human action

**Every** **dashboard** **brief** must **include** **at** **least** **one** **safe,** **role**-**appropriate** **human** **action** **unless** **no** **such** **action** **exists** **(e.g. ** **pure** **compliance**-**lockout** day **/ ** **full** **OOO** with **escalation** **documented) **. **

If **exempt,** the **brief** **must** **state** **why** in **one** **line** **(no** **shame,** **no** **fake** **tasks) **. **

---

## 5. “15-minute human tasks” (examples; no PII)

- **Text** one **volunteer** **lead** **: ** **confirm** they saw **yesterday’s** **ask** **. ** ** **  
- **Send** one **short** **host** **thanks** *draft* to **MCE** **for** **review** **(not** **auto**-**send) **. ** ** ** **  
- **Log** one **beta** **follow**-**up** **as** **“** **needs** **detail** **”** **in** the **governed** **path** **(5H) **. ** ** ** **

---

## 6. “1-hour relationship block” (examples; no PII)

- **Three** **check**-**in** **calls** to **county** **contacts** **(scripted** **,** **no** **lists** on **unsecured** **device) **. ** ** ** **  
- **Attend** **/ ** **host** a **brief** **listening** **convo** **(see** 5D/5E) **+ ** **one** **task** to **log** **takeaways** **(aggregate,** not **a** **transcript) **. ** ** ** **
