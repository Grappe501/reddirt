## Main email body (prepared for Steve / MCE approval)

Dear Kelly,

We’re **beginning the website review and Ask Kelly onboarding** we outlined in the manual: you’ll be **walked through the public site one page (or section) at a time** — at a pace that works for you.

**What you can do in each review session**

- **Approve** copy that still sounds like you, or **rewrite** a line so it does.  
- **Flag** something that needs legal, comms, or finance sign-off before it goes public.  
- **Add context** the team should know (tone, a story, a nuance) so it can land in the **admin packet** cleanly, not in side texts.

**How Ask Kelly will work with you**

- Ask Kelly will start with **plain, short questions** first, then go **deeper as you’re comfortable** (see the progressive plan — you’re not getting the full question bank in one sitting).  
- You can **skip** a question; your **progress checklist** is there to help, not to rush you. **Sessions can be short.**  
- **Every meaningful change** becomes a **clean admin packet** for the team — not a jumble in chat.  
- **High-impact** changes will show **downstream impact** (what else might need to change) **before** anything sensitive publishes. **Nothing sensitive will publish on its own** — you have **MCE+comms** and the **approval** path we already agreed in the **matrix**.

**What to expect**

- A **progress checklist** in plain language (not internal readiness scores).  
- **Edits in your own words** are welcome; the team will **integrate approved changes** into the site and comms in the right order.  
- If something isn’t right yet, that’s what the **packet** is for — we fix it in workflow, not in a hurry on the public page.

**Start here (pick one to open first)**

- **“Start with the Home page”**  
- **“Review the first section you care about most”**  
- **“Tell Ask Kelly what sounds like you — and what doesn’t”**  

We’re proud of the direction. This process is to make the **public site** and **Ask Kelly** **sound unmistakably like your service to Arkansas — safely and clearly.**

Thank you,  
[Name / MCE as approved]
