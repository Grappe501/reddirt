## 1. Why some people waited (narrative)

- **Some** **signed** **up** **months** **ago** **(or** **earlier) **. ** The campaign did **not** want to **fully** **lift** **up** the **volunteer** **network** before the **organization** was **ready** to **move** with **clarity** and **care** **. ** ** **  
- **Time** **was** **spent** **building** **relationships,** **strengthening** **brand** **awareness,** **raising** **the** **resources** needed, **and** **laying** a **responsible** **foundation** for **the** work **ahead** **. ** **  
- This **reactivation** **frame** **is** **honest** **and** **grateful** **—** not **a** **defensive** **spiral,** not **a** **transactional** “we forgot you” **pivot** **. **

---

## 2. Now frame (movement, not a one-off)

- **Now** is the time to **start** the **movement** toward **electing** Kelly.  
- The **movement** should **leave** **organized** **communities** **stronger,** not **hollow,** when **the** **work** is **sustained** **. ** **  
- The **goal** is **communities** **gaining** **leadership** **capacity** **and** **power** **in** the **hands** of **the** **people** **—** not **a** **flash**-**in**-**the**-**pan** **volunteer** **sprint** **. **

---

- **Grateful** **;** **acknowledge** **early** **sign**-**ups** **with** **respect** **. ** ** **  
- **Honest** about **sequencing;** no **invented** **urgency** or **invented** **metrics** **. ** ** **  
- **No** **apology** **spiral** (no **excessive** **self**-**flagellation) **. ** ** ** **  
- **No** **overclaim** of **readiness,** **polls,** or **omniscient** product **(see** `SYSTEM_READINESS_REPORT` **). ** ** ** ** **  
- **Movement**-**building,** not **transactional** **“** **hours** **for** **swag** **”** **framing** **. ** ** ** **  
- **No** **public** “**AI**” **as** a **product** **name** **—** use **Ask** **Kelly** **,** **Campaign** **Companion,** **guided** **help** **(see** **5E/5H) **. **
