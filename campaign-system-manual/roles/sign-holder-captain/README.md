# Role manual — Sign holder / visibility captain

## 1. Role purpose
Coordinate **lawn/road** **visibility** in **geography**-scoped way without **doxxing** **homes** (see `PRECINCT_SIGN_HOLDER...` **workflow**).

## 2. Why this role matters
**Low cost**, **high** **identity** in **neighborhoods**; **feeds** **OIS** only as **%** and **safety**-checked stories.

## 3. Where this role sits
Under **field**; peers with **precinct** captain where both exist; **GOTV**-adjacent.

## 4. Who this role serves
**Field** and **comms** for **turf** look; **public** with **aesthetic** discipline.

## 5. Who supports this role
**V.C.** (people), **finance** (print), **compliance** (RO**W**/ordinance), **safety** point.

## 6. Dashboard used
**Tasks** + (future) **GOTV**/visibility **tiles**; **no** public **map** of **addresses**.

## 7. Manual sections
`PRECINCT_SIGN_HOLDER...`, Ch. 22, 10–11, **phase** 13 **lifecycle** doc.

## 8. First 24 hours
**Legal** one-pager for **jurisdiction**; **PPE**/night **visibility** rules; **1** **route** **lead**.

## 9. First 7 days
**Materials** list + **stake** **inventory**; **captain** bench per **precinct** **string** key (aggregate).

## 10. First 30 days
**%** of **target** **precincts** with a **booked** **shift**; **replan** for gaps.

## 11. Daily workflow
**Dispatch** in **GOTV**; **earlier** **cycle**: **1–2** **coordination** **passes**/week.

## 12. Weekly workflow
**Field** and **GOTV** lead sync; **comms** for **“we’re visible”** **social** (approved).

## 13. KPIs
**%** prec with **any** sign **window**; **incidents**; **waste** (unused signs).

## 14. Workbench tasks
`CampaignTask` {kind sign_route}; `VolunteerAsk` for **neighbors**; **intakes** for **donations** of **stake** time.

## 15. Approval authority
**Local** **safety** **stop**; **not** **content** in **contrast** **ads** without **comms**.

## 16. Training modules
**Traffic** and **de-escalation**; **trespass** **avoidance**; **no** **arguments** in **yards** without **RBI**.

## 17. Tools used
Route **sheets** (offline) + **tasks**; **media** to **comms** only through **SOP** (photos w/ **consent** where needed).

## 18. Common mistakes
**Dox** maps; **stealing** **signs** from **opponents**; **taking** **photos** of **private** **yards** w/o consent for **public** post.

## 19. Escalation path
**Field** **mgr**; **compliance** if **cited**; **safety** if **threats**.

## 20. Growth path
**GOTV** lead; **regional** **if** program scales.

## 21. Election Day
**AM** sign **check**; **evening** **visibility** to **SOP**; **escalation** to **GOTV** for **voter** **issues** (not the captain’s to fix w/o training).

## 22. Missing system features
**First-**class **sign** program **object**; **permit** **registry**; **OIS** **tile** w/ real **%** (needs **clean** data).

## 23. Current readiness level
**1–2** (manual+tasks); **4** for **if** SOP+tasks **executed** well **off**-product.
