# Role manual — Owner

**Pass 4 book-style playbook:** [owner.md](../../playbooks/roles/owner.md)

## 1. Role purpose
Policy, final authority, break-glass, vendor and legal posture for the Campaign Operating System — not the default daily Workbench triage unless the campaign is a solo or tiny operation.

## 2. Why this role matters
Without clear owner, field and comms will fork; PII and money risk go unmanaged.

## 3. Where this role sits
Above CM; can deputize; cannot outsource counsel sign-off in-app.

## 4. Who this role serves
Candidate, staff, and voters through trustworthy execution.

## 5. Who supports this role
Counsel, treasurer, and (in practice) a trusted admin for keys — never the same as “all-powerful in Workbench by default” without intention.

## 6. Dashboard used
All admin; owner should be able to read `SYSTEM_READINESS_REPORT` and `MANUAL_PASS_2_COMPLETION_REPORT` for truth in planning.

## 7. Manual sections
Ch. 0, 12, 15–19, 20–22, `MANUAL_INFORMATION_REQUESTS_FOR_STEVE` (this role fills many lines).

## 8. First 24 hours
Confirm RACI: money, comms, crisis, PII, press — written, not in DMs; P0: unreviewed public contrast claim in flight.

## 9. First 7 days
Access matrix: who is ADMIN; when keys rotate; who owns Netlify/DB vendors.

## 10. First 30 days
30d risk register: PII, money, **public “AI”** language accidents on Guided product, OIS overclaim, GOTV.

## 11. Daily workflow
GOTV+ED: high touch; else: 1x CM sync, legal/finance as calendar demands.

## 12. Weekly workflow
Readiness and manual Pass cadence; sign-off on public numbers in OIS if you take that responsibility.

## 13. KPIs
Organizational health (not vanity), cash runway with finance, time-to-legal on flags, number of PII near-misses (should go to 0 with training).

## 14. Workbench tasks
As needed if you are operator — not ideal; prefer CM. Owner decisions may spawn `WorkflowIntake` to document.

## 15. Approval authority
Final; including break-glass on data exports, spend, and strategic narrative shifts that affect compliance.

## 16. Training modules
Crisis, PII, finance high-level, press, “what the Guided Campaign System is allowed to do” in public (not “AI” as brand).

## 17. Tools used
Entire app in policy; do not use author-studio outputs as volunteer-facing without comms+compliance path.

## 18. Common mistakes
Solo-acting in DMs; skipping treasurer on in-kind; letting “demo” OIS be quoted as VAN in press.

## 19. Escalation path
If you are the top: counsel; outside counsel; law enforcement for threats — off-app, not a ticket in GitHub with PII.

## 20. Growth path
Next cycle: template org for Arkansas Dems; not automatic from this app.

## 21. Election Day
RACI with candidate+CM+comms+compliance; you sign only what policy says you sign; no surprise press.

## 22. Missing system features
Owner dashboard for risk only (separate from CM queue) if desired; typed `OwnerPolicy` object in DB — future.

## 23. Current readiness level
5 for “human owner exists + team”; 2 for “product enforces owner-only gates” — policy mostly lives off-app today.
