# Role manual — New volunteer

## 1. Role purpose
First meaningful action after **`POST /api/forms`** response: shift, contact, or training — before a **Pathway** label sticks.

## 2. Why this role matters
Volume and quality of **first** activations determine whether the **Guided Campaign System** (public name) has anything real to work with.

## 3. Where this role sits
Bottom of the ladder; feeds **P5**, field, and **WorkflowIntake** completion as tasks close.

## 4. Who this role serves
The campaign’s capacity and the **county/team** that requested help.

## 5. Who supports this role
**Volunteer coordinator** primary; field secondary.

## 6. Dashboard used
`get-involved` and email/SMS. **`/dashboard`** is **PH** — not a completed product (Pass 2A).

## 7. Manual sections
Ch. 3–4, 5 (optional), 7 (tasks as assigned), 23 (Pathway).

## 8. First 24 hours
Confirm **contact**; one **concrete** next step; privacy acknowledgment.

## 9. First 7 days
One **completed** action (shift/relational/learning) or explicit reschedule.

## 10. First 30 days
Pick **P5** or **solo Power of 1** or **turf** path with V.C. — documented in `ROLE_MANUAL_INDEX`.

## 11. Daily workflow
Check asks/tasks only when active; do not demand daily login until **`/dashboard`** is real.

## 12. Weekly workflow
1 touchpoint to coordinator if still ramping; optional training.

## 13. KPIs
First action time; **show** rate; **return** for second shift.

## 14. Workbench tasks
**Operators** may create **`CampaignTask`**, **`VolunteerAsk`**; volunteer does **not** use Workbench.

## 15. Approval authority
None for **voter** data or comms; event rules from field.

## 16. Training modules
**Organizing Guide** intro; **data minimization** on phones.

## 17. Tools used
Web forms, email, SMS, optional **relational** app — scope per product.

## 18. Common mistakes
Collecting **PII** off-program; over-promising on **VAN**-like list access (not public).

## 19. Escalation path
V.C. → field mgr → **CM** for safety or conflict.

## 20. Growth path
**P5** member, **event** helper, or **P1** sustained solo.

## 21. Election Day responsibilities
**Execute** assigned slot; report **no-show** through **field** only.

## 22. Missing system features
Authenticated **first-action** tiles; “we received you” without exposing `WorkflowIntake` IDs.

## 23. Current readiness level
**3** — public intake is **5**; **volunteer** **home** is **2**.
