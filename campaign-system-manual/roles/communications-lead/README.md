# Role manual — Communications lead

**Pass 4 book-style playbook:** [communications-lead.md](../../playbooks/roles/communications-lead.md)

## 1. Role purpose
Own channel mix, calendar, and comms workbench health — co-parent of Message Engine and Narrative distribution with message lead and NDE lead.

## 2. Why this role matters
Keeps a single public voice; prevents off-message posts that break compliance and OIS numbers story.

## 3. Where this role sits
Reports to CM; leads message lead and NDE lead when those roles are separate; hand to compliance/counsel on edge cases.

## 4. Who this role serves
Voters (through message), field (through scripts and timing), press (earned) per RACI.

## 5. Who supports this role
Comms drafters, social admin, style hub, email queue operators, (optional) external counsel.

## 6. Dashboard used
Comms workbench: plans, drafts, sends, broadcasts, email queue, media, media monitor, narrative distribution (coordination).

## 7. Manual sections
Ch. 8–9, 20–22, COMMS-UNIFY-1 foundation, language audit (internal vs public terms).

## 8. First 24 hours
Kill-switch check on auto sends; P0: pending broadcast with legal/ finance flag.

## 9. First 7 days
14-day comms map; owner of review queue for draft ownership; R who signs each channel in crisis SOP.

## 10. First 30 days
Message calendar tied to MCE/NDE where live; retrain on “no AI in public” product naming (Guided Campaign System).

## 11. Daily workflow
AM: queue; crisis days: 3x+ check on social/press; EOD: handoff if 24/7 split.

## 12. Weekly workflow
OIS gap review with NDE+field (aggregate only); one pass on county narrative with county-facing staff.

## 13. KPIs
Cycle time, defect rate, retraction count, on-time major sends, channel health (internal).

## 14. Workbench tasks
`CommunicationPlan` with `sourceWorkflowIntakeId` when applicable; `CampaignTask` for ship blockers; email workflow items.

## 15. Approval authority
Comms and channel routing; not legal alone on contrast; not voter export; coordinates with owner/candidate on press per RACI.

## 16. Training modules
Crisis comms, FEC/ethics (where applicable), MCE+NDE docs, public vocabulary sheet.

## 17. Tools used
Comms workbench, SendGrid/Twilio via env (never paste secrets in manual); social workbench, narrative admin.

## 18. Common mistakes
Letting “AI” appear in public UI copy; shipping without ContactPreference gating; bypassing NDE for “just one post.”

## 19. Escalation path
CM; compliance/counsel; owner for crisis brand; data for segments that look like microtargeting in field UI.

## 20. Growth path
Chief of comms; principal; sideways to campaign manager in small orgs.

## 21. Election Day
RACI-locked comms; rapid response to CM+owner+compliance; no unilateral contrast hits.

## 22. Missing system features
End-to-end narrativeWaveId on every plan; full telemetry to “what shipped” in all geographies.

## 23. Current readiness level
4–5 for comms models and UI; 3 for NDE+MCE+OIS as one provable line without manual reconciliation.
