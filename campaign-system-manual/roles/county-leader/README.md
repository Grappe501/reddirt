# Role manual — County leader

**Pass 4 book-style playbook (same org role; index filename):** [county-coordinator.md](../../playbooks/roles/county-coordinator.md)

## 1. Role purpose
**Cultural+strategic** point for a **county** in **public** and **ops**; **not** a **micromanager** of **VAN** in app (no public VAN).

## 2. Why this role matters
Counties are the **ladder** for Arkansas; **OIS**+**county** **page** are **spines** of **Field Intelligence** story.

## 3. Where this role sits
Between **regional** and **city**/captains; **touches** `County` in DB via **staff** tools when granted.

## 4. Who this role serves
**Volunteers** and **voters** in **county**; **state** w/ **honest** **rollup**.

## 5. Who supports this role
**Regional** organizer, **field** mgr, **V.C.**, **data** on **targets** (aggregate).

## 6. Dashboard used
Public `/counties/[slug]`; **admin** `counties` **editors** if assigned; OIS (read) for **NWA**/peer story.

## 7. Manual sections
Ch. 9–12, 11, 22, `COUNTY_...` plan, gap analysis (policy, not in repo).

## 8. First 24 hours
**3**-objective **quarter** **focus**; **1** P0 to **V.C.** for **triage**; **1** to **comms** for **local** **hook**.

## 9. First 7 days
**Huddle** w/ every **captain** **tier** you have; **OIS** **read** with awareness of **demo** vs **live** tiles.

## 10. First 30 days
**Roster** **health** of **leads**; **GOTV** **predraft**; **fundraiser**/event cadence w/ fin policy.

## 11. Daily workflow
**GOTV**-adjacent; else **1** **huddle** **slot**/day max — avoid burnout.

## 12. Weekly workflow
**Regional** **call**; **V.C.** / **field** **sync**; **county** **intel** (staff) for **one** “so what” **move**.

## 13. KPIs
Pipeline, **event** **capacity** **honest**, **P5** **anchors** w/in county (when wired), **0** PII leaks.

## 14. Workbench tasks
`WorkflowIntake` w/ `countyId` when you’re assignee; **not** for **voter** **rows** w/o data policy.

## 15. Approval authority
**Field**-scoped; **$** w/ fin; **public** “attack” line only w/ **comms**+**compliance** path.

## 16. Training modules
`DATA-1` **ethics**; `COUNTY` **intel** SOP; **OIS** **honest** **missing** data training.

## 17. Tools used
Public county; OIS; **admin** if granted; **ask** in **P5** product when exists.

## 18. Common mistakes
**Rogue** comms; **bypassing** **regional** on **$**; **OIS** as **VAN** on phone for random volunteers.

## 19. Escalation path
Regional → **field** **mgr** → **CM**; **legal** on **Oppo**; **owner** on **turf** **split** w/ other county.

## 20. Growth path
Regional; **deputy** **state** surrogate programs if applicable.

## 21. Election Day
**County** **GOTV** **command w/in** SOP; **one** comms line through **GOTV**+**comms**; no **lone** **local** “we won” w/o **compliance** for **sourcing**.

## 22. Missing system features
**Single** `CountyIntelligenceViewModel` across /counties+OIS+briefings; **P5** **county** **rollup** in **product**.

## 23. Current readiness level
**4** on **public**+**admin** **shell**; **2** on **unified** **truth**+**P5** in **one** place.
