# Role manual — Power team leader

## 1. Role purpose
**Coach** a **P5** **team** roster (consent-scoped) and **follow-up** latency.

## 2. Why this role matters
Multiplies P5; reduces **V.C.** and **field** support load on **repeat** nudges.

## 3. Where this role sits
Between **P5** **member** and **precinct**/**city**; **OIS** in **rollups** when wired.

## 4. Who this role serves
Team **members** and **turf** health (agg).

## 5. Who supports this role
**Field**; **V.C.** for **personality** **conflict**; **compliance** for **harassment** reports.

## 6. Dashboard used
**PH** ` /dashboard/leader`.

## 7. Manual sections
Ch. 5–6, 14, `tasks` SOP, 23.

## 8. First 24 hours
Roster; **1** nudge; **1** follow-up.

## 9. First 7 days
Weekly team expectation; **pipeline** to **captain** if capacity.

## 10. First 30 days
**Stability** metrics; handoff doc for if leader travels.

## 11. Daily workflow
2 short **nudges** in **final** **month**; else **2–3**/week.

## 12. Weekly workflow
1:1s as needed; **OIS** **read** of **turf** **aggregate** (public-safe).

## 13. KPIs
Completion %, **follow-up** **latency** (internal), **healthy** **invite** mix.

## 14. Workbench tasks
**V.C.** can assign **you** a **task**; **not** for **P5** **members**.

## 15. Approval authority
**Team**-scoped; **not** comms/exports; **not** VAN.

## 16. Training modules
**Coaching**, **de-escalation**, **consent** **refresh**.

## 17. Tools used
(Planned) leader UI; for now: **comms** off-channel w/ V.C. policy on **PII** in text.

## 18. Common mistakes
**Pushing** on **P5** when someone must demote to **P1**; public **roster** posts.

## 19. Escalation path
Field mgr → V.C. → **CM** on **structural** issues.

## 20. Growth path
**Precinct**/**city** captain; sideways to **event** if logistics sweet spot.

## 21. Election Day
Own **P5** **GET**-out w/in **GOTV**; report **gaps** to field.

## 22. Missing system features
Roster + **permissions**; **OIS** **leader** view **TBD**.

## 23. Current readiness level
**2** product; **3** w/ SOP+offline coaching.
