# Role manual — Event lead (program)

## 1. Role purpose
Own **event** density, **quality**, and **no-show** recovery across counties — **not** every single `CampaignEvent` click.

## 2. Why this role matters
Events are where **P5** **recruits** and **ID**- **friendly** public touch happen; they anchor **Narrative distribution** in **place**.

## 3. Where this role sits
Between **field** and **comms**; **pairs** with **V.C.** for staffing.

## 4. Who this role serves
**Volunteers** (good shifts), **public** (safe venues), **CM** (predictable program).

## 5. Who supports this role
**Field** (turf), **compliance** (permits, COVID/venue policy if any), **finance** (deposits/costs).

## 6. Dashboard used
`admin/events*`, `workbench/calendar`, public `/events*`.

## 7. Manual sections
Ch. 10, 22, **lifecycle** phase **4–7**, 12.

## 8. First 24 hours
**Crisis** pass on **imminent** events; one **noshow** risk resolved.

## 9. First 7 days
**2-week** look-ahead; **host** SOP; **comms** pack for each **tier**.

## 10. First 30 days
**Rubric** for **event** ROI (honest) + **V.C.** **pipeline** to fill rosters.

## 11. Daily workflow
**Morning** look at `CampaignEvent` **sync** and **suggestions** (community).

## 12. Weekly workflow
**Regional** / **county** **huddle**; **GOTV** line **integration** in final 30d.

## 13. KPIs
Fill rate, **no**-show %, new **vols** / event, **incident** count.

## 14. Workbench tasks
`CampaignEvent` + **linked** `CampaignTask` for “need host”, “need AV”.

## 15. Approval authority
**Budget** to **preapproved** line items per **owner**; **not** new **palm card** line without comms+compliance.

## 16. Training modules
**ADA**, **safety**, **youth** rules at schools if applicable; **de-escalation**.

## 17. Tools used
`CampaignEvent` model; Google calendar sync; **orchestrator** for **suggested** **events** (if used).

## 18. Common mistakes
Over-promising **headcount**; **voter** file **access** to **volunteer** (forbidden in public product).

## 19. Escalation path
**Field** **mgr**; **compliance** (venue, liability); **CM** (cancel/postpone).

## 20. Growth path
**Field** **manager**; sideways to **GOTV** in **final** window if strong **ops** pattern.

## 21. Election Day
**Rally** / **watch** party plan per **RACI**; **decompress** and **data** to **GOTV** team.

## 22. Missing system features
**Single** “event” **to** `VolunteerAsk` **auto-**generator from **OIS** when capacity is low (future).

## 23. Current readiness level
**4** — `CampaignEvent` **mature**; **cross-**OIS **auto**-fill **1–2**.
