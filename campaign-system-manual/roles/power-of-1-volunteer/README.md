# Role manual — Power of 1 (solo contributor)

## 1. Role purpose
Sustained hours **without** building a P5 team — valid path for time-limited or intro volunteers.

## 2. Why this role matters
Captures capacity that would otherwise churn when **P5** is not a fit; keeps **honest** labor in the system.

## 3. Where this role sits
Parallel to P5; still rolls to **Field Intelligence** aggregates via **tasks** and **events**.

## 4. Who this role serves
Same as volunteer — community outcome first.

## 5. Who supports this role
V.C. and **field** (assignments, not “therapy” in the app).

## 6. Dashboard used
**PH** `/dashboard` — not production-complete.

## 7. Manual sections
Ch. 4–5, 10, 14 (KPIs honest), 23.

## 8. First 24 hours
Clarify **hours/week** and **channel**; one scheduled block.

## 9. First 7 days
Complete **one** week block; log via coordinator’s process.

## 10. First 30 days
30d hour target set with **V.C.**; no shame if under — adjust.

## 11. Daily workflow
Punch in/out to task expectations only when events/shifts are active; else async.

## 12. Weekly workflow
**Check-in** with V.C. for next week’s **asks**.

## 13. KPIs
**Hours** or **deliverables** (table shifts, data entry within policy) — not “leaderboard vs others.”

## 14. Workbench tasks
**Staff**-created **asks**; volunteer sees comms, not **Workbench**.

## 15. Approval authority
None; cannot approve **sends** or **exports**.

## 16. Training modules
**Organizing Guide** for role-specific (tabling, phone bank policy).

## 17. Tools used
`VolunteerAsk`, email, in-person; **voter** tools **n/a**.

## 18. Common mistakes
Comparing self to P5 team metrics; ghosting without telling V.C.

## 19. Escalation path
V.C. → field → CM.

## 20. Growth path
**P5** or **neighborhood** captain if desired; sideways to **comms** support.

## 21. Election Day
Assigned shift only; no improvisation on **poll** rules.

## 22. Missing system features
**P1** is not a first-class object in Prisma — use **tags** in **tasks/asks** until modeled.

## 23. Current readiness level
**2** (policy exists in manual; product tags **TBD**).
