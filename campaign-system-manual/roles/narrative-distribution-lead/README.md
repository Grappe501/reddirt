# Role manual — Narrative distribution lead (NDE)

**Pass 4 book-style playbook:** [narrative-distribution-lead.md](../../playbooks/roles/narrative-distribution-lead.md)

## 1. Role purpose
Own **Narrative distribution** **waves**: **reach**, **checklists**, and **readiness** across channels — *not* the same job as “write the sentence” (message lead) alone.

## 2. Why this role matters
Prevents “approved copy that never **ships**” and coordinates **geography**-scoped story beats with **MCE** (Message Engine).

## 3. Where this role sits
Between **comms** strategy and **field**; peers with **message** lead; approves *routing* not *law*.

## 4. Who this role serves
**State**, **regional**, **county** **need** in public narrative.

## 5. Who supports this role
**Message** lead (language), **CM** (priority), **compliance** (sensitive), **data** (no microtargeting language).

## 6. Dashboard used
`/admin/narrative-distribution` + **`CommunicationPlan`** where linked. **OIS** for **gaps** **panels** (verify demo vs real).

## 7. Manual sections
Ch. 8–9, 20–21, `MESSAGE_CREATION...` **workflow**.

## 8. First 24 hours
**Pipeline** read; **P0** items with **stuck** “ready” that did not ship; channel owners named.

## 9. First 7 days
**Wave** plan for 2–3 weeks; **RACI** for owners per channel.

## 10. First 30 days
**Narrative** **calendar** tied to `CommunicationPlan` **metadata** (when present) + field milestones.

## 11. Daily workflow
**Grid** for channel readiness; flag **P0** **stalls**; no **bulk** public posting as NDE (execution stays on rails).

## 12. Weekly workflow
**Field**-originated **gaps** review (**aggregate** feedback only); pass **MCE** updates to message lead.

## 13. KPIs
**Ship** rate, **time** from approved **packet** to **last mile**, **rework** on errors.

## 14. Workbench tasks
`CampaignTask` for localization; `WorkflowIntake` for **stories**; **comms** **plans** with `sourceWorkflowIntake` — traceability.

## 15. Approval authority
**Routin**g and **re-ship**; **not** unilateral on **contrast** **claims** (counsel).

## 16. Training modules
MCE+NDE **docs**; **Field Intelligence** *aggregate* **language** for **public** copy.

## 17. Tools used
`lib/narrative-distribution/*` types; NDE **components**; **comms** **send** (execution).

## 18. Common mistakes
Confusing NDE for **VAN**; **volunteer**-facing “AI” language — **forbidden** in public; use **Guided** terms.

## 19. Escalation path
**Comms** lead → **CM** → **owner**; **legal** to **compliance** on risky lines.

## 20. Growth path
**Comms** director track; sideways to **message** lead (smaller orgs, one person).

## 21. Election Day
**Narrative** **hourly** if crisis; else **GOTV** line-of-day locked **pre-ED** when possible.

## 22. Missing system features
**One** `narrativeWaveId` in every `CommunicationPlan` + **telemetry** on **ship** per channel in all regions.

## 23. Current readiness level
**3–4** — **admin** UI and **types** exist; **maturity** of **OIS** **panels** varies.
