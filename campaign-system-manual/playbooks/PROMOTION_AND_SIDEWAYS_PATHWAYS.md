# Promotion and sideways pathways — Pass 4

**Purpose:** Honest, non-binding career ladders for volunteers and part-time leads. **No** implied hiring, salary, or title until owner/CM confirms. Vocabulary: Organizing Guide, Pathway, Workbench.

**Principles**
- Lateral moves (sideways) are valid: depth in one county or one channel beats fake “up” everywhere.
- Readiness = training modules + OIS/operator sign-off on access, not self-grants.

| From (archetype) | Sideways (same level, new craft) | Toward (next rung, examples) | Gates |
|------------------|----------------------------------|-----------------------------|-------|
| New volunteer / Power of 1 | block captain, sign holder, event helper | Power of 5 member, local host | task completion, PII attestation |
| Power of 5 member | different turf or program (youth, faith) | P5 team lead / house party captain | M-001..M-005, field manager line |
| County neighborhood lead | comms or calendar helper if cleared | county coordinator, road team | county mapping + MI §4 |
| Trained canvasser / surrogate bench | data hygiene volunteer | V.C., surrogate coord | M-010+, no uncounseled claims |
| Fundraising assist | visibility or events | call time asst, postcard lead (copy path) | treasurer rules, **no** unsourced $ claims |
| Campus / youth | NAACP or focus category (with training) | youth student coordinator (policy) | parent rules, C review at edges |
| Trainer / coach path | comms or field specialist | training director (owner scope) | certification ladder, MI §33 |

**Break-glass:** Owner may slot someone into a *temporary* acting role (documented in operator queue) without implying permanent title.

**Conflicts:** If two people claim the same county or channel, **CM** + **field** decide; escalate to **owner** for public-facing title.
