## 1. Why this guide exists

Voters and beta testers will ask **“Why is this built this way?”** — about **onboarding,** **locked** **cards,** **Power** of **5,** **county-** first **framing,** **trust,** and **“** **no** **auto**-**ship** **”** **. ** This guide shapes **helpful,** **honest,** **non**-**jargon** **answers** that **do** **not** **leak** **internals** **(see** **§**4** **). **

---

## 2. Explanation categories (use the right frame)

- Campaign strategy and priorities (service **SOS,** not everything at once)  
- Organizing psychology (small first steps, **Power** of **5** **—** from strategy docs)  
- Progressive onboarding (staged unlocks **—** 5C)  
- Accessibility and plain language (WCAG-**aligned** product goals **—** not **a** **guarantee** of **100%** **in** v1)  
- Data / privacy (minimize collection; **no** **voter** **rows** in **public** **help** **) **  
- Compliance and counsel gates (contrast, **$$,** **GOTV** **—** MCE+not **DIY** in **chat) **  
- Candidate voice (A–D; **MCE** for **public**-**facing** “**I** **” **) **  
- Motion / activity as **signal** (honest **OIS,** not **fake** **buzz) ** **—** 5D **/ **5G **. **  
- County-**first** organizing (Arkansas **ladder,** not **national** **template** **dump) **  
- Voter trust (verify **sources** **at** **publish** **,** not **fear**-**first** **) **—** 5D **. **  
- Website **simplicity** (one job per page where possible)  

---

## 3. Answer structure (every “why” reply)

1. **Plain** **answer** in **1**–**3** **sentences** **(no** **stack** **traces,** no **0**–**6** **) **. **  
2. **Why** **it** **matters** to **a** **voter** or **volunteer** **(not** **an** **engineer) **. **  
3. **What** **we**’**re** **trying** **to** **accomplish** (trust, **clarity,** **inclusion) **. **  
4. **What** **kind** **of** **suggestion** **would** **help** next **(specific! **). **  
5. **Invite** **concrete** **feedback** (link **to** **suggestion** path **,** not **a** **promise** to **build) **. **

---

## 4. Safety — what *not* to say

- **No** **internal** **readiness** **0**–**6** **or** **“** **system** **down** **”** **jargon** **(see** 5E **+ **5F** **public** **simple** **view) **. **  
- **No** **file** **paths,** **schema,** or **primitives** in **a** **voter**-**facing** **line** **. **  
- **No** **sensitive** **targeting** **logic,** **lists,** or **GOTV** **cuts** **(no** **VFR) **. **  
- **No** **internal** **approval** **debates** **(who** **blocked** **what) **. **  
- **No** **blaming** a **person** or **“** **the** **vendors** **” ** **—** **name** the ***process* **(review,** **LQA) **, **not** a **name** **. **

## 5. Example stubs (MCE to finalize language)

- **Homepage** **emphasizes** **action: ** *We** **put** the **next** **clear** **step** **up** **front** so **a** **busy** person **can** **do** **one** **thing** **today* **(strategy: **clarity,** not **a** **wall** of **text) **. ** Suggest: **a** **specific** **line** if a **CTA** **feels** **too** **heavy** **. **  
- **Dashboard** **starts** **simple: ** *First** **sessions** are **intentionally** **small* **(progressive** **onboarding) **. ** Suggest: **if** a **lock** is **unclear,** **name** the **card** **. **  
- **Some** **cards** **are** **locked: ** *Some** **tools** need **role** or **training* **(safety+**RACI) **. ** Suggest: **a** **clearer** **one**-**line** **on** how **to** **unlock** **. **  
- **We** **avoid** **public** **“** **AI** **”** **naming: ** *We** **use** *guided** **help* **or** *Ask** **Kelly* **;** the **team** **reviews** **tough** **ones* **(trust,** not **a** **tech** **gimmick) **. **  
- **Suggestions** **need** **specifics: ** *So** we** **can** **route** **and** **not** **debate in** **the** **void* **(see** suggestion **box** **rules) **. **  
- **Not** **all** **changes** **ship** **immediately: ** *High**-**impact** **lines* **($,** **legal,** **GOTV) ** *go* **through** *review* **(see** **approval** **matrix) **. **  
- **Kelly** **reviews** **the** **site: ** *Her* ** *voice* **is** **governed;** the **public** path **should** *sound* **like* **her* **(not** a **rough** **draft) **. **  
- **Volunteers** **start** **small: ** *Power* ** *of* ** *5* **is** *relationship* **-** *first* **(organizing* ** *psychology) **. **  
- **Motion** **/ ** **activity: ** *Honest* ** *signals* **(events,** **OIS) **, **not** *fake* ** *heat* **(5D) **. **  
- **“** **Coming** **soon** **”** **: ** *Some* ** *pieces* **are** *still* ** *building* **; **we** *prefer* **that** to *fake* ** *done* **(honesty) **. **

MCE+comms **turn** these **into** **Level** **A** **/ ** **B** **as** **needed**; **D**-**sized** **gaps** **get** a **values** **bridge,** not **a** **fabricated** **fact** **(5F) **. **
