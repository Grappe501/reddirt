## 1. Retention doctrine

People **stay** when they **feel** **useful,** **respected,** **trained,** **thanked,** and **not** **overwhelmed** **(see** `THANK_YOU_...` **+ **`ADVANCE_...` **+ **`HUMAN_...` **).** **

---

## 2. Low-turnover principles

- **Right**-**sized** **tasks** **(see** `ADVANCE_...` **windows) **. ** ** ** ** ** **  
- **Clear** **expectations** on **the** **claim** **landing** **(§**5 in **`ADVANCE_...` **). ** ** ** ** ** ** **  
- **Advance** **notice**; **sparing** use of same-day** **/ ** **urgent** **ping** to **the** **same** **person** **(§**3 below) **. ** ** ** ** ** ** **  
- **Meaningful** work **(not** **busywork) **. ** ** ** ** ** ** **  
- **Recognition** **(thanks,** **not** a **shout**-**out** without **consent) **. ** ** ** ** ** ** **  
- **Role** **mobility** **(pathway,** **P5,** **training) **. ** ** ** ** ** ** **  
- **No** **shame** **dashboards;** **no** **peer**-**visible** **ranking** of **volunteers** **(5C) **. ** ** ** ** ** ** **  
- **No** **burnout** **“** **hero** **”** **culture**; **escalation** before **resentment** **(see** `ADVANCE_...` **) **. **

---

## 3. Engagement** **metrics (design; **no** **invented** **targets** in **repo) **

Track **in** **aggregate** and **RACI**-**safe** **slices,** not **a** **voter** **row: **

- **Task** **claim** **rate,** **completion** **rate,** **time**-**to**-**claim,** **time**-**to**-**complete** ** ** ** **  
- **Reactivation** **/ ** **repeat** **engagement,** **7**-**day** **/ ** **30**-**day** **retention** **(definitions** = **SOP) **. ** ** ** ** ** ** ** **  
- **Satisfaction** **pulse** (opt**-**in,** **anonymize**-**by**-**default) **. ** ** ** ** ** ** ** **  
- **Decline**-**with**-**reason** **patterns** (capacity **vs** **mismatch,** not **a** **label** on **a** **person) **. ** ** ** ** ** ** ** **  
- **Burnout** **risk** **flags** (e.g. **repeated** **urgent** **to** **same** **assignee) **; **triage** to **V.C. **+ **CM** **(see** `ADVANCE_...` **) **. **

**KPI** **targets** = **owner**+**V.C. **+ **MI** **(§**48** **);** **this** **file** does **not** set **numeric** **goals** **. **

## 4. Volunteer** **health** **statuses** (internal** **;** **not** a **shipped** **label** in **a** **public** **UI** without **policy) **

| Status | Intuition |
|--------|-----------|
| **New** | Onboarding **/ ** first **touches** **. ** ** |
| **Active** | **Steady** **cadence,** **completing** **/ ** **claiming** **. ** ** |
| **Warming** | **Lighter** **touch**; **re**-**engagement** **path** **. ** ** |
| **At** **risk** | **Dropping** **/ ** **silence;** **V.C. ** **+ ** **not** a **shame** **list** **. ** ** ** |
| **Paused** | **Intentional** **rest,** **life**-**first;** **respected** **. ** ** ** |
| **Re**-**engage** **gently** | **Low**-**pressure,** **opt**-**in,** not **a** **spray** **. ** ** ** |
| **Leadership**-**ready** | **P5,** **county,** **training**-**gated,** not **a** **free** **GOTV** **tool** **(RACI) **. ** ** |

- **Escalation** before **resentment** (see `ADVANCE_...` **+ **`ESCALATION_PATHS` **) **. **
