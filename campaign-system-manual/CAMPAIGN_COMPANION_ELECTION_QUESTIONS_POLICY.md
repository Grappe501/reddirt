# Campaign Companion — election and trust questions policy (Manual Pass 5D)

**Lane:** `RedDirt/campaign-system-manual`  
**Type:** Policy for **guided help** — Campaign **Companion**, **Pathway Guide**, **Field Guide** — not “AI” as public product language (`USER_FRIENDLY_WORKBENCH_UX_REQUIREMENTS.md`, `GUIDED_REPORT_BUILDER_AND_ASSISTED_QUERY_SYSTEM.md`).  
**Doctrine:** `ELECTION_CONFIDENCE_TRANSPARENCY_AND_GET_UNDER_THE_HOOD_DOCTRINE.md` · `KELLY_PUBLIC_TRUST_TALKING_POINTS_AND_FAQ.md`

**Product honesty:** Companion does **not** provide **legal** finality; counsel for edge cases. Any internal model or NLP is **not** labeled “AI” in public copy.

---

## 1. Scope: what Companion may do

- Point to **primary** public sources: **Arkansas SBEC** (e.g. 2024 post-election audit **PDF** on `sbec.arkansas.gov`), **SOS** materials, and the **MIT Election Data and Science Lab** **Elections Performance Index (EPI)** with **current** link (see MI §41 for “approved” URL list).  
- Give **short** **plain-English** definitions (from doctrine) separating **election administration**, **voter fraud**, **petition / canvass** process issues, **isolated** local misconduct, **machine/process** error, and **public confidence** concerns.  
- Deep-link to **vetted** **website** **FAQ** and **static** **talking** **points** — **not** **mint** new **unreviewed** **statutory** or **fraud** **claims** in a live chat bubble.

**Out of scope** for unmediated public auto-answer:

- **Legal** advice, **chronicling** a **live** **criminal** **case** by name, or naming **victims** or **suspects** in ways counsel has not approved.  
- **Any** **new** number (percent, count) not in the **pre-approved** clip-file or website (`KELLY_PUBLIC_TRUST_TALKING_POINTS_AND_FAQ.md` §7).

---

## 2. Tone (secure, never dismissive)

- **Match** `ELECTION_CONFIDENCE_TRANSPARENCY_AND_GET_UNDER_THE_HOOD_DOCTRINE.md` **§5**.  
- **No** ridicule of skeptics, **no** “just stop worrying,” **no** “only paranoid people care.”  
- **No** **invented** **SOS** **powers** (`MANUAL_INFORMATION_REQUESTS_FOR_STEVE.md`).

---

## 3. Response patterns (archetypes)

| User need | Good pattern | **Do not** |
|-----------|----------------|------------|
| “Is the whole system rigged?” | Short **vetted** answer from `KELLY_...` §5 + link to FAQ + link to **SBEC** primary | New statistics; “no fraud anywhere ever”; conflating **alleged** and **proven** |
| “What’s in the 2024 audit?” | “**Read** the official **SBEC** **PDF**; the report describes (match source wording on hand count sample vs. tabulator totals) …” + direct link | Paraphrase that **tightens** or **dilute**s the state’s language without human review |
| “What is EPI?” | EPI = **administration** benchmark (MIT), not a fraud test + link to current EPI | Tie a **state** **EPI** **rank** to **fraud** **guilt** or **innocence** |

---

## 4. Escalation to humans

- **Press,** **legal,** or **alleged**-crime: `playbooks/ESCALATION_PATHS.md` — Companion points to **official** / **comms** **contact**, not a **hot** take.  
- **MCE** / **NDE:** Any **new** public **line** = **LQA** pipeline, not autogenerated Companion text (`SEGMENTED_MESSAGE_AND_DISTRIBUTION_SOP.md`).

## 5. Logging and safety (when product ships)

- Log **topic** (e.g. `election_trust`) for rubric, **not** PII.  
- No experiments that stoke **fear** or pressure users to **mishandle** data (`USER_FRIENDLY_WORKBENCH_UX_REQUIREMENTS.md`).
