## 1. Why use a “game” mental model (professionally)

Field org is **competitive** in **outcomes** but must **not** be **adversarial** to **safety** and **compliance** **(see** `USER_FRIENDLY_WORKBENCH_UX_REQUIREMENTS` **“**no** **pressure**-**to**-**mishandle**-**data**” **).** A **staged,** **learnable** **system**—like mastering a **complex** **competitive** **format** with **well**-**written** **rules**—helps **onboarding** and **mentorship** without **dumbing** **down** the **seriousness** of **the** **work**.

## 3. Fun, empowering, and safe (together)

- **Fun** = **mastery,** **clarity,** **and** **team** **huddle**—**not** pranks, **trolling,** or **speed**-**at**-**all**-**costs.  
- **Empowering** = “**I** **can** see **the** next **unlocked** **step** and **who** **to** **ask** for **the** next **RACI;** I’m **not** **blocked** in **silence** **.”**  
- **Safe** = **LQA,** **PII,** and **$** are **tournament** **rules** you **do** **not** **rewrite** in **a** **private** **chat** for **a** **badge** **.  

---

## 4. The “infinite ladder” (always more to grow)

- **There** is **no** “**you**’ve** **maxed** **the** **campaign,**’” **in** a **serious** **org**—there are **deeper** **Pathways,** new **tournament** **seasons** (GOTV, **ED,** runoffs), and **mentorship** to **teach** **(see** `TRAINING_MODULE_INDEX` **L5–L7) **.  
- The **OS** should **visually** **and** **procedurally** **signal** that **mastery** = **serving** **others** and **hardening** **systems,** not **hoarding** **access** **(owner** still **LQA,** not **Solo** **Rogue** **Admin** **) **.  

**Product honesty:** **“**Infinite** **ladder**” **=** design **+** **culture** **+** **training**; **the** **repo** **may** not **graph** this **in** a **product** **yet**—see **readiness** **file** **.
