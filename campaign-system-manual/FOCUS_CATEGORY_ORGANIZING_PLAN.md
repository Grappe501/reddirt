# Focus category organizing plan (Pass 3E)

**Lane:** `RedDirt/campaign-system-manual` · **Markdown only** · **2026-04-27**  
**Public language:** **Campaign Companion**, **Guided Campaign System**, **Organizing Guide**, **Field Intelligence**, **Message Engine**, **Campaign Operating System**, **Workbench**, **Pathway Guide** — not “AI.”

**Doctrine:** **Relationship**, **listening**, **alignment**, and **service** — **not** “these groups are our base” unless **true** and **governed**. **No** invented endorsements. **Civic and community** groups are **not** **assumed** **supporters** from a single touch.

---

## 1. Executive summary

**Focus categories** are **lenses** for **outreach**, **message**, **travel**, **county** **ladder**, and **(when** **permitted) **endorsement** / **quote** **lanes** — **without** conflating **category** with **partisan** **allegiance**. This document covers **Extension Homemakers (EHC)**, **homemakers / caregivers / family economy**, and additional **policy**-aligned **categories** with **category-to-message**, **travel**, **endorsement (process)**, and **county** **ladder** maps, plus **dashboards**, **risks**, and **Steve** list.

**Extension Homemakers (EHC) — public facts to cite carefully:** Arkansas Extension Homemakers Council is described in official materials as a **statewide** membership network — figures in the wild include **3,200+** members and **320+** clubs, with another UA System Division of Agriculture / Extension page citing **3,700+** members and **320+** clubs. **Use** a **conservative** **bracket** in internal planning: **“3,200+** **members,** **320+** **clubs”** **unless** **Steve** **confirms** a **single** **current** **stat** for public copy. **Extension** has **offices** in **all** **75** **counties** (UA System Division of Agriculture) — use for **local** **access** design, not as “we have relationships in all 75” unless **true**.

---

## 2. Purpose of focus categories

- **Route** comms, **MCE/NDE**, and **Field Intelligence** to **true** **local** **hooks**.  
- **Sequence** **travel** with **county** **activation** and **P5** / **host** **funnels**.  
- **Avoid** **one**-**size**-**fits**-**all** **messaging** that **erases** **rural,** **caregiver,** and **youth** **context**.

---

## 3. Extension Homemakers (EHC) category

- **Statewide** civic and **community** network; **county**-level **structure**.  
- **Leadership** **development** and **service** (align with **Pathway**).  
- **Respectful,** **non**-**transactional** **first** **touches** — **listen**-**first** at **fairs** and **county** **Extension**-adjacent **events** where **appropriate** (compliance for **public** **facilities**).  
- **No** **assumption** of **universal** support; **EHC** **is** not a **monolith**.

---

## 4. Homemakers / caregivers / family economy category

- **Economic** **stress**, **time** **poverty**, and **civic** **participation** **barriers** — message with **dignity**; **no** **stereotyping**.  
- **Tie** to **house** **party** and **festival** **calendar** (Pass 3E travel).  
- **Youth** **caregivers** and **minors** — **safety** **rules** from youth plan.

---

## 5. Working-class / worker protection category

- **Alignment** on **transparency** and **access**; **labor** **mechanics** and **contrast** **on** **policy** only with **sourcing** (strategy manual, **no** **unsourced** **opponent** **claims**).

---

## 6. Students / youth category

- **See** `YOUTH_CAMPUS_AND_STUDENT_ORGANIZING_PLAN.md` — **separate** **ladder** and **safety** **rules**.

## 7. Veterans / military families category

- **Respect**-**first;** **separate** **compliance** for **certain** **event** **types** (federal, facilities). **No** **stolen** **valor**-adjacent **imagery** or **claims**.

## 8. Faith and community category

## 9. Small business / local economy category

- **SOS** **services** to **filers** and **transparency** **affect** **business** **confidence** — **factual,** not **demonizing** other sectors.

---

## 10. Direct democracy / ballot access category

- **How** **measures** **qualify**; **transparency** in **ballot** **administration** — **law**-**accurate** **only**.

## 11. Transparency / accountability category

- **Core** **SOS** **lane**; **MCE/NDE** default **safety** in **citations**.

## 12. Rural infrastructure category

- **Polling** place **reality,** **postal** / **rural** **EV** **nuance** — **document** in **OIS** when **sourced**; **travel** plan **reaches** **rural** **fairs** (Pass 3E).

## 13. Category-to-message map (design)

| Category | MCE/NDE angle (non-exhaustive) | Guardrail |
|----------|-------------------------------|-----------|
| EHC / caregivers | **Service,** **community,** **local** **ties** | No **paternalism**; **no** **fake** **“we’re** **in** every **club**” |
| Working-class | **Access** **=** **time** and **dignity** | **No** **unattributed** **contrast** |
| Students | **Civic** **power,** **future** **pipeline** (opt-in) | **Youth** **safety** |
| Veterans | **Trust** in **administration** of **honor**-related **systems** (only if **factual** scope) | **No** **militarized** **imagery** without **context** |
| Faith | **Common** **good,** **integrity** of **public** **process** | **Compliance** **+** **venue** **RACI** |
| Small business | **Filing,** **registry** **usability,** **transparency** | **Not** a **lobbying** **promise** for **deregulation** **without** **policy** **review** |
| Direct democracy / transparency | **Process** **integrity** | **No** false **certainty** on **measures** |
| Rural | **Access** to **voting,** **information** | **Sourced** **infrastructure** **facts** only |

- **OIS** “**heat**” **map** that **overstates** **depth** of **category** work.
