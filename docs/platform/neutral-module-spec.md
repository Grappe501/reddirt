# Platform modules — civic participation (neutral spec)

Use for **engineering tickets**, vendor docs, or contexts that need **descriptive** (not persuasive) language.

## Scope

Structured support for:

- Citizen-led ballot initiatives and referendums *(where law allows)*  
- Civic engagement pathways  
- Education on state-level participation  

## Module 1 — Referendum / petition opt-in network

- User opt-in to notifications about referendums and petition participation  
- Capture: signup, geography (ZIP/county), engagement events *(privacy policy required)*  

## Module 2 — Ballot initiative pipeline

- Issue identification workflow  
- Coalition alignment *(internal)*  
- Legal pathway mapping *(counsel)*  
- Signature collection tracking *(compliance-aware)*  

## Module 3 — Civic education layer

Content types:

- How referendums work in Arkansas  
- How ballot initiatives work  
- How citizens participate in state governance  

Formats: articles, short modules, FAQs.

## Module 4 — Youth civic participation (exploratory)

- Informational resources on models from other jurisdictions  
- Policy pathway notes *(not legal advice)*  
- Staging area for future initiative work  

## Module 5 — Labor & workforce hub *(optional)*

- Educational content on labor frameworks and workforce trends  
- Coalition hooks and future initiative framing *(as needed)*  

## Site integration (neutral)

- Nav cluster: **Civic Power** / **People’s Process**  
- Subpages: Referendums; Ballot initiatives; Civic education; Youth participation; Workforce & labor *(if enabled)*  
- Conversion: stay informed; participate in initiatives; learn the process  

---

This document does not replace legal review for petitions, fundraising, or electoral communications.
