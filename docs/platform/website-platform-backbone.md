## Governing philosophy

The Arkansas Secretary of State should be a steady constitutional officer focused on service, transparency, trust, and participation. The office touches elections, public records, business and nonprofit filings, the State Capitol, and civic education. The unifying idea is simple: government belongs to the people.

## Platform spine

- Protect the right to vote.
- Serve every county fairly.
- Restore trust through transparency.
- Keep elections local, with state support and accountability.
- Defend direct democracy and ballot access.
- Modernize elections and filings responsibly.
- Expand civic education.
- Support Arkansas businesses and nonprofits with clear, predictable service.
- Protect the official record.
- Make the State Capitol feel like the People's House.

## Tone

- Calm constitutional stewardship
- Service over spectacle

- Local officials as partners
- Civic education as an invitation, not a lecture
