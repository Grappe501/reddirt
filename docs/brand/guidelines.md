# Brand guidelines (v1.0)

## Identity statement

**A working-class Arkansas movement rooted in place, built on dignity, and focused on participation.**

## Brand tension (design for this)

- Old-school trust ↔ new system building  
- Local identity ↔ statewide scale  
- Calm strength ↔ quiet rebellion  

## Emotional signature

> “This feels like something real—and I can be part of it.”

**Core feeling:** *people like me built this* — grounded, strong, familiar, honest, capable; **not** flashy, aggressive, or ideological in aesthetic.

## Positioning type

**Regional civic movement** — place-based, participation-focused; not a generic national party brand or activist cliché.

### Brand anchors

1. **Place-based identity** — geography and shared experience over abstract labels.  
2. **Work and contribution** — labor and participation over status cues.  
3. **Stability + change** — continuity with values; room for reform.  
4. **Participation over ideology** — process-forward framing.

## Logo system (directions)

### Primary — grounded wordmark

```
RED DIRT
DEMOCRATS
──────────
```

- Serif or strong slab; **RED DIRT** emphasized; thin horizontal rule (ground).  
- Use: header, official docs.

### Secondary — horizon mark

- Thin horizon line, subtle rising arc or sun; calm future / stability.  
- Use: sections, graphics, headers.

### Tertiary — monogram **RDD**

- Geometric, condensed.  
- Use: favicon, mobile nav, avatars.

**Rules:** clear space; no stretch; prefer cream or transparent backgrounds; avoid busy photo overlays.

**Avoid:** donkey icon, flag-heavy clip art, aggressive protest stock, partisan gimmicks.

## Color psychology (summary)

- **Red Dirt** — earth, labor, foundation; warm, not aggressive.  
- **Deep Soil** — seriousness, trust.  
- **Cream Canvas** — warmth, approachability.  
- **Washed Denim** — workwear familiarity; stability without “party blue.”  
- **Field Green** — growth, local economy.  
- **Sunlight Gold** — hope, warmth, emphasis.

*(Exact values: [`design-tokens.md`](design-tokens.md).)*

## Typography psychology

Serif headlines = **trust, legacy**; sans body = **clarity, now**.  
Together: *we respect the past; we operate in the present.*

Alternates if needed: **Merriweather** (headings), **Source Sans 3** (body).

## Components (personality)

**Buttons — primary**

- Background: Red Dirt; text: Cream; radius 8px; padding ~12px 20px.  
- Hover: slight darken + small lift (`translateY`).

**Cards**

- Cream or white; 12px radius; soft shadow; generous padding.

**Navigation**

- Minimal; sticky; transparent → solid on scroll.

**Sections**

- Alternate Cream Canvas with **slightly** tinted bands; backgrounds always full-width.

## Imagery

**Prefer:** real Arkansans, real places, natural light, work and community contexts; slight warmth; mild desaturation leaning earth-tone.

**Avoid:** obvious stock grins, staged political clichés, generic skylines, diversity-as-prop.

## Motion

Subtle, purposeful; fade + lift; card hover. No bounce or startup-hype patterns. Respect `prefers-reduced-motion`.

## UX psychology

**Order:** validate → invite → convert.  
**Never:** shame, assume alignment, overwhelm.

**Engagement model:** orient → explore → opt-in → deepen.

**Microcopy:** prefer pathways over pressure—e.g. “Find your place” alongside direct donate/volunteer where appropriate.

### Example CTAs / headers

- Join the Movement · Find Your Place · Stay Connected · Get Involved · Start Locally  
- Form headers: “Let’s stay connected,” “Start where you are,” “Be part of what’s next”

### Signature phrases (cross-channel)

- Built from the ground up  
- Power back to the people  
- A place to land  
- Not spectators. Builders.  
- Rooted in Arkansas  

## Voice — use / avoid

**Use:** plainspoken language, everyday concepts, process framing, “build a system that works.”

**Avoid:** partisan slogans, insider jargon, pure rage without clarity, consultant polish that reads fake.

## What breaks this brand

- Generic Democrat blue/red template  
- Corporate nonprofit “annual report” aesthetic  
- Activist cliché graphics  
- Narrow centered layouts (see [`layout-requirements.md`](layout-requirements.md))  
- Over-polished tone that hides the grassroots truth  

## Visual signature lines

Use sparingly as anchors:

- **Built from the ground up.**  
- **Power back to the people.**
