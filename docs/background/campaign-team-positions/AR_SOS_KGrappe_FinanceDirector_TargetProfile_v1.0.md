Internal Campaign Memo: Finance Director – Kelly Grappe for Secretary of State

Campaign Philosophy & Culture

The Kelly Grappe for Secretary of State campaign is a mission-driven movement built on trust, community engagement, data-informed strategies, and equity-driven solutions. Every team member plays a multi-functional role rooted in team cohesion and shared accountability. The Finance Director must be both strategic and relational—driven by results but grounded in integrity and people-first values.This role will evolve with campaign milestones. Early on, the Finance Director will wear multiple hats (development, compliance, donor relations, planning). As the campaign scales, the position will specialize and oversee a growing team responsible for fundraising, events, donor cultivation, digital revenue, and reporting.

Role Overview

The Finance Director oversees all fundraising strategy, execution, and donor engagement. This includes building and managing a multi-tiered fundraising program—from grassroots donors to high-net-worth individuals—while ensuring all reporting and legal compliance. The position works in partnership with the Campaign Manager and Strategic Director.

Preferred Experience

- 2+ cycles as Finance Director or senior fundraising role- Experience with ActBlue, NGP VAN, and donor database systems- Direct donor solicitation (calls, meetings, event hosting)- Familiarity with campaign finance laws and state campaign finance compliance- Demonstrated ability to raise $2M+ in multi-channel environments

Leadership Progression

The Finance Director reports to the Campaign Manager and coordinates closely with the Strategic Director. This role sits at the center of campaign sustainability and strategic planning, evolving into a department head role by Phase 3 with direct reports in compliance, research, digital fundraising, and event coordination.
