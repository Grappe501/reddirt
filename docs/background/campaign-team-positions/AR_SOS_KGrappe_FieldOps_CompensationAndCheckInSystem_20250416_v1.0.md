Campaign Personnel Engagement & Compensation System

1. Personnel Check-In System (“Digital Clock-In”)

Purpose:Create a transparent and real-time system to track availability, project work, and time logged for ALL paid and volunteer personnel across the campaign.

System Features:- Web/mobile-based interface for check-in/check-out.- Users select from a dropdown menu of tasks/projects they are working on.- Optional status toggles: "Working on project," "Available for assignment," or "Offline."- Timestamped activity logs.- Leadership dashboard to view:  - Active/available personnel by role and geography  - Hours worked daily, weekly, monthly  - Work type distribution (comms, field, ops, etc.)  - Automated alerts if team member reaches 10hrs, 40hrs, or 50hrs thresholds

Access Management:- The campaign can restrict login access for users once time limits are met, roles are paused, or budgets are maxed.- This allows active control over total personnel costs while preserving engagement and fairness.

Usage Requirements:- All campaign members (paid and volunteer) check in daily when active.- This system enables field control, staff accountability, and compliance for payroll/timekeeping.

2. Compensation Chronology Tied to Campaign Milestones

Base Philosophy:Start lean and scale smart. Recruit people at the volunteer level where possible, and reward early participation with escalating compensation based on activity level and campaign revenue benchmarks.

Compensation Tiers:1. Initial Onboarding (Volunteer): All staff begin as volunteers.2. 10-Hour Threshold: Switch to hourly rate at 20% discount from low salary range.3. 40-Hour Threshold: Convert to full-time salary position at lower end of scale.4. 50-Hour Threshold: Salary bump + OT bonus (time and a half structure).

Financial Milestone Bonuses:- $10 Million Raised:  - All paid personnel receive individual bonus pool distribution.  - Progressive bonus model: Based on hours contributed + tenure + responsibility level.- Over $10 Million:  - Higher bonus % (ex: 20% of overage split among full team).  - Hourly and salary ranges increase another 10–15%.  - Adds campaign wellness and flexibility benefits.

3. Personnel Engagement Philosophy

- Volunteer-First Model:  - Encourages buy-in from early staff.  - Builds a performance-based pathway to employment.  - Protects budget in early phase while building momentum.

- Leadership Incentives:  - Directors onboard as volunteer-leads first.  - Directors receive first pay once managing team + 40hr minimum.  - Bonuses tied to performance, milestones, and team outcomes.

- Transparency + Equity:  - Staff and volunteers know the ladder, timelines, and thresholds.  - Bonus pools are announced and distributed based on time + contribution.  - No team member left behind as we scale.
