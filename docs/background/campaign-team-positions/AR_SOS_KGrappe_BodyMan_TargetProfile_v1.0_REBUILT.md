The Kelly Grappe for Secretary of State campaign is a mission-driven movement emphasizing trust, momentum, local leadership, and adaptability. Role Overview

The Body Man serves as the Candidate's closest aide, providing comprehensive logistical and personal support to ensure the Candidate's daily activities run smoothly. This role encompasses managing schedules, coordinating travel, preparing briefing materials, and addressing immediate needs, allowing the Candidate to focus on campaign objectives.
