Campaign Philosophy & Culture

The Kelly Grappe for Secretary of State campaign is a mission-driven movement emphasizing trust, momentum, local leadership, and adaptability. Every team member is expected to operate as part of a cohesive, mission-focused team. The Campaign Manager is the operational linchpin of the campaign, responsible for executing the strategic vision set forth by the Strategic Director and the Candidate.
