Finance Director – Kelly Grappe for Secretary of State

Campaign Philosophy & Culture

The Kelly Grappe for Secretary of State campaign is rooted in community-first leadership, data-driven decision-making, and bold, values-based strategy. Every team member plays a multi-functional role built on trust, adaptability, and a commitment to results. The Finance Director is a cornerstone of this operation, managing campaign sustainability and investor confidence from day one through Election Day.

Role Overview

The Finance Director oversees all aspects of campaign fundraising, budgeting, and compliance. They are responsible for building a comprehensive donor program—grassroots and major donors—and ensuring all financial activity is properly recorded and reported. This position works closely with the Candidate, Campaign Manager, and Strategic Director to develop strategic fundraising goals and align financial planning with campaign phases.

Key Responsibilities

- Develop and implement a robust, multi-tiered fundraising strategy- Manage the campaign’s call time program, including prepping, staffing, and follow-up- Identify, cultivate, and maintain donor relationships at all levels- Oversee all contribution tracking, acknowledgments, and donor compliance- Coordinate digital fundraising and recurring donor campaigns- Produce regular fundraising reports, cash flow projections, and budget forecasts- Plan and execute fundraising events and bundler programs- Ensure full compliance with Arkansas campaign finance regulations and reporting requirements

Qualifications

- 2+ cycles of campaign or nonprofit development leadership experience- Proven ability to meet or exceed major fundraising targets ($2M+ preferred)- Strong grasp of campaign finance law and compliance systems (NGP, ActBlue, etc.)- Excellent communication skills and donor stewardship instincts- Highly organized and deadline-driven with strong analytical abilities

Compensation & Progression

This position follows a milestone-based growth plan:| Phase   | $ Raised     | Compensation & Role Evolution                                                ||---------|--------------|--------------------------------------------------------------------------------|| Phase 0 | $0–$100K     | Volunteer – builds system, drafts strategy, compiles lists                     || Phase 1 | $100K–$500K  | Paid hourly – manages call time, launches grassroots fundraising               || Phase 2 | $500K–$1.25M | Full-time salaried – hosts events, directs major donor outreach, compliance    || Phase 3+| $1.25M+      | Department head – builds and manages team; oversees all campaign finance ops   |

Leadership Structure

The Finance Director reports to the Campaign Manager and works closely with the Strategic Director and Candidate. As the campaign grows, this position will expand to oversee a small team including compliance staff, digital fundraising leads, and donor research assistants.
