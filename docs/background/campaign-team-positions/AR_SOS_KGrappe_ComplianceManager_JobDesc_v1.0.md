Compliance Manager – Kelly Grappe for Secretary of State

Campaign Philosophy & Culture

The Kelly Grappe for Secretary of State campaign is built on transparency, accountability, and grassroots power. As we scale toward a $10M+ fundraising goal, our success depends not only on our ambition but on our integrity. The Compliance Manager will be our watchdog and our systems-builder—ensuring every dollar is tracked, every deadline is met, and every report is accurate.

Role Overview

The Compliance Manager is responsible for overseeing the campaign’s financial reporting, disbursement documentation, contribution tracking, and adherence to all Arkansas campaign finance laws and reporting rules. They will work in close partnership with the Finance Director, Campaign Treasurer, and any external legal or compliance consultants.

Key Responsibilities

- Prepare or support preparation of all state campaign finance filings and submission deadlines- Track incoming contributions to ensure compliance with limits and source regulations- Maintain real-time donor and disbursement databases in NGP VAN- Manage vendor contracts, payment records, and reimbursement documentation- Ensure backup documentation for audits or reviews- Support internal controls for spending and approvals

Qualifications

- 2+ years in campaign, legal, or nonprofit compliance or finance roles- Experience with state campaign finance filings, ActBlue, and NGP or equivalent- Strong attention to detail, confidentiality, and data integrity- Ability to work independently and meet recurring deadlines- Bonus: Experience handling reporting for large campaign budgets ($1M+)

Compensation & Progression

This position follows a milestone-based growth model:| Phase   | $ Raised     | Compensation & Role Evolution                                  ||---------|--------------|------------------------------------------------------------------|| Phase 0 | $0–$100K     | Volunteer – systems setup, contribution tracking, policy review  || Phase 1 | $100K–$500K  | Paid hourly – assists with state campaign finance filings, limits, disbursements    || Phase 2 | $500K–$1.25M | Full-time salaried – leads all compliance systems/reporting      || Phase 3+| $1.25M+      | Audit readiness, expanded legal role, internal training systems  |

Leadership Structure

The Compliance Manager reports to the Finance Director and works closely with the Campaign Manager, Treasurer, and external consultants. This position is essential for maintaining campaign credibility and financial stability at all stages.
