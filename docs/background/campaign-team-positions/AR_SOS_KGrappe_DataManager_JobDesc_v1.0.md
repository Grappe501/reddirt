Data Manager – Kelly Grappe for Secretary of State

Campaign Philosophy & Culture

Smart campaigns run on good data. The Data Manager makes sure every field shift, fundraising appeal, and volunteer action is grounded in numbers that matter. This role helps guide decision-making at every level of the campaign and ensures we're always working smarter—not just harder.

Role Overview

The Data Manager is responsible for managing VAN/NGP systems, targeting, list generation, and reporting across campaign departments. They build voter contact universes, maintain data cleanliness, and create dashboards to track progress to goal. This role requires both technical ability and a team-oriented mindset to deliver insights that move people to action.

Key Responsibilities

- Maintain and manage VAN/NGP structure and user access- Build and distribute walk lists, phone banks, and targeting universes- Track field and digital performance metrics weekly- Coordinate targeting with Field, Digital, and Communications departments- Clean, sort, and analyze voter and supporter data for campaign planning- Train staff and volunteers on data best practices as needed

Qualifications

- 2+ cycles or 3+ years in campaign data, analytics, or operations- Proficiency in VAN/NGP, Excel/Google Sheets; bonus if familiar with SQL or GIS- Experience with voter targeting, reporting, and performance tracking- Ability to communicate technical findings to non-technical teammates- Organized, reliable, and deadline-oriented with strong attention to detail

Compensation & Progression

This role follows a milestone-based structure:| Phase   | $ Raised     | Compensation & Role Evolution                                ||---------|--------------|----------------------------------------------------------------|| Phase 0 | $0–$100K     | Volunteer – helps build VAN setup, list infrastructure         || Phase 1 | $100K–$500K  | Paid hourly – manages list targeting, data cleanup, and reports|| Phase 2 | $500K–$1.25M | Full-time salaried – leads targeting and analytics dashboarding || Phase 3+| $1.25M+      | Oversees precinct-level targeting, modeling, and optimization  |

Leadership Structure

The Data Manager reports to the Strategic Director and works closely with Field, Digital, Finance, and Communications. They are responsible for ensuring the accuracy, accessibility, and strategic use of all campaign data.
