Operations Associate – Kelly Grappe for Secretary of State

Campaign Philosophy & Culture

Every campaign needs glue—the behind-the-scenes teammate who makes sure everyone has what they need. The Operations Associate helps us deliver materials, prep for events, and manage field office logistics so the campaign can keep growing and winning every week.

Role Overview

The Operations Associate provides hands-on support for the campaign’s logistics, supplies, field office setup, and event execution. They work directly with the Operations Director and support cross-team needs including deliveries, signage, materials inventory, and travel prep.

Key Responsibilities

- Assist with supply runs, print jobs, and delivery logistics- Help set up and tear down events across Arkansas- Support field office operations and restocking of materials- Manage inventory of printed materials, yard signs, and campaign gear- Coordinate with field, volunteer, and events teams to ensure resource access- Provide day-of-event logistical support including signage, AV setup, and packet prep

Qualifications

- Organized, reliable, and task-oriented- Ability to lift 25–50 lbs and travel locally throughout Arkansas- Strong communication and time management skills- Experience with spreadsheets and Google Workspace tools preferred- Campaign, nonprofit, or service industry logistics experience a plus

Compensation & Progression

This position follows a phased growth model:| Phase   | $ Raised     | Compensation & Role Evolution                               ||---------|--------------|---------------------------------------------------------------|| Phase 0 | $0–$100K     | Volunteer – basic errands, signage drop-off, setup support   || Phase 1 | $100K–$500K  | Paid hourly – delivery, logistics, and resource coordination  || Phase 2 | $500K–$1.25M | Full-time salaried – manages supplies and logistics flow      || Phase 3+| $1.25M+      | Supports GOTV site logistics and manages regional needs       |

Leadership Structure

The Operations Associate reports directly to the Operations Director. This role works across departments to ensure campaign infrastructure is strong, functional, and adaptive to growth.
