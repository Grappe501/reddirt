People Operations Manager – Kelly Grappe for Secretary of State

Campaign Philosophy & Culture

Our campaign culture is intentional: people-first, mission-aligned, and accountability-focused. The People Operations Manager ensures that the internal experience of our team reflects the values we campaign on—equity, transparency, inclusion, and strategy with heart.

Role Overview

The People Operations Manager will lead human resources and staff development systems across the campaign. They manage hiring and onboarding processes, document policies, support team cohesion, and ensure we meet compliance for employment practices. This role is both strategic and relational—balancing systems and support for a growing team.

Key Responsibilities

- Coordinate all onboarding and offboarding processes for staff and volunteers- Maintain internal policies, team handbook, and hiring documentation- Track personnel records and coordinate performance review processes- Mediate personnel conflicts and support internal team health- Collaborate with department heads to build supportive and equitable workflows- Lead internal staff meetings, culture-building check-ins, and transition planning

Qualifications

- 3+ years experience in human resources, nonprofit operations, or team development- Familiarity with campaign or grassroots team dynamics preferred- Strong communication, policy-writing, and interpersonal skills- Demonstrated commitment to equity, inclusion, and staff care- Comfort working in fast-paced, evolving team environments

Compensation & Progression

This role follows a phase-based model:| Phase   | $ Raised     | Compensation & Role Evolution                                     ||---------|--------------|--------------------------------------------------------------------|| Phase 0 | $0–$100K     | Volunteer – drafts handbook and onboarding templates               || Phase 1 | $100K–$500K  | Paid hourly – coordinates hiring and onboarding                    || Phase 2 | $500K–$1.25M | Full-time salaried – leads HR policy and people ops system         || Phase 3+| $1.25M+      | Oversees personnel strategy and manages team culture planning      |

Leadership Structure

The People Operations Manager reports to the Campaign Manager and works cross-departmentally. This position becomes essential to team cohesion and long-term sustainability—ensuring people are supported as the campaign grows.
