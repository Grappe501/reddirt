Campaign Philosophy & Culture

The Kelly Grappe for Secretary of State campaign is a mission-driven movement emphasizing trust, momentum, local leadership, and adaptability. Every team member operates as part of a cohesive, mission-focused team. We are not merely hiring for jobs; we are inviting individuals into a strategy built on shared values and collective goals.All team members initially wear multiple hats, with duties evolving as we achieve funding milestones and expand our personnel. The Field Director plays a pivotal role in this structure, requiring flexibility, resilience, and a commitment to the campaign's overarching mission.

Role Overview

The Field Director is responsible for developing and executing the campaign's grassroots organizing strategy. This includes managing field staff and volunteers, coordinating voter outreach efforts, and ensuring effective community engagement. The Field Director works closely with the Campaign Manager and Strategic Director to align field operations with the overall campaign strategy.
