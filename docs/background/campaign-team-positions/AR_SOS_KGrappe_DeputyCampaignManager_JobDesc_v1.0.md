Deputy Campaign Manager – Kelly Grappe for Secretary of State

Campaign Philosophy & Culture

This campaign is powered by strategy and sustained by operations. The Deputy Campaign Manager helps lead that engine—ensuring departments work in sync, decisions are executed, and the whole campaign moves forward every day with clarity and speed.

Role Overview

The Deputy Campaign Manager supports the Campaign Manager in daily operations, supervising key departments and internal coordination. They serve as the principal executor of campaign strategy across teams, resolve day-to-day logistical issues, and uphold campaign discipline across the board.

Key Responsibilities

- Lead cross-department coordination and communication- Manage daily operations and resolve execution bottlenecks- Supervise department heads and ensure alignment with campaign plan- Organize internal staff check-ins, leadership huddles, and timelines- Serve as on-call problem solver for staff, volunteers, or logistical breakdowns- Assist with hiring, onboarding, and culture building

Qualifications

- 2+ cycles in campaign or large-scale organizing management- Excellent people management, communication, and decision-making skills- Experience with field, ops, or campaign-wide execution strategy- Ability to multitask, stay calm under pressure, and resolve conflict professionally- Deep understanding of grassroots movement culture

Compensation & Progression

This role follows a growth-tied model:| Phase   | $ Raised     | Compensation & Role Evolution                                    ||---------|--------------|-------------------------------------------------------------------|| Phase 0 | $0–$100K     | Volunteer – supports internal planning and coordination           || Phase 1 | $100K–$500K  | Paid hourly – manages daily internal ops and supports execution   || Phase 2 | $500K–$1.25M | Full-time salaried – supervises staff and manages key departments || Phase 3+| $1.25M+      | Manages daily execution and campaign-wide coordination            |

Leadership Structure

The Deputy Campaign Manager reports to the Campaign Manager and serves on the senior leadership team. They directly supervise assigned department heads and provide weekly strategy updates, summaries, and decisions-in-motion to the campaign’s top leadership.
