Digital Director – Kelly Grappe for Secretary of State

Campaign Philosophy & Culture

The Kelly Grappe for Secretary of State campaign is a people-powered, tech-savvy movement built to scale. We believe in data-informed action, storytelling that moves people, and a digital-first mindset that meets voters where they are. The Digital Director will be the architect of that vision—turning content into power, strategy into momentum, and clicks into voters.

Role Overview

The Digital Director is responsible for leading the campaign’s digital strategy across email, SMS, social media, online fundraising, and digital advertising. They work in close partnership with the Communications and Finance teams to create engaging, consistent, and high-performing online campaigns. This is a high-impact role requiring creativity, efficiency, and a sharp eye on both message and metrics.

Key Responsibilities

- Manage the campaign’s email and SMS calendar, including list growth, engagement, and segmentation- Create, test, and optimize digital fundraising appeals in collaboration with the Finance team- Lead content strategy across all social media platforms- Track digital KPIs across all channels; adjust tactics based on results- Manage ad buys (in-house or through vendors) and report performance weekly- Design and manage rapid-response communications for digital audiences

Qualifications

- 2+ cycles or 3+ years in political or nonprofit digital strategy- Proven results managing multi-channel campaigns with fundraising, organizing, or persuasion goals- Proficiency with tools like ActBlue, ActionNetwork, NGP, Mobilize, Meta Business Suite, Spoke, and CRM platforms- Strong writing, editing, and project management skills- Experience with digital advertising or vendor coordination a plus

Compensation & Progression

This position follows a growth-based model:| Phase   | $ Raised     | Compensation & Role Evolution                                     ||---------|--------------|-------------------------------------------------------------------|| Phase 0 | $0–$100K     | Volunteer – email drafts, basic graphics, platform audit           || Phase 1 | $100K–$500K  | Paid hourly – regular email/SMS/social execution, platform growth  || Phase 2 | $500K–$1.25M | Full-time salaried – leads digital calendar, strategy, and testing || Phase 3+| $1.25M+      | Oversees contractors, ads, reporting, and creative direction       |

Leadership Structure

The Digital Director reports to the Communications Director and Campaign Manager and collaborates closely with the Finance and Field teams. This position will ultimately lead a team of digital contractors, manage budgets, and ensure digital targets are met or exceeded.
