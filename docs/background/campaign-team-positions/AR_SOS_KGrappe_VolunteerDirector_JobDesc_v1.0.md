Volunteer Director – Kelly Grappe for Secretary of State

Campaign Philosophy & Culture

This campaign is built on people power. From county fairs to front porches, volunteers are the voice and face of our movement. The Volunteer Director builds and leads a program that turns supporters into organizers, neighbors into captains, and energy into impact.

Role Overview

The Volunteer Director is responsible for overseeing the recruitment, onboarding, training, engagement, and retention of campaign volunteers. They build systems to track involvement, manage weekly shifts, and ensure that volunteers are supported, celebrated, and strategically deployed. They work closely with Field, Operations, Digital, and Events teams to connect people with purpose at scale.

Key Responsibilities

- Manage weekly recruitment and onboarding systems- Design and lead training programs and leadership development- Build and maintain communication flows with active and prospective volunteers- Track volunteer activity and match capacity to field/event needs- Build and support precinct-level captains and team leads- Manage Mobilize, VAN, and relational organizing tools to track performance

Qualifications

- 2+ cycles or 3+ years in volunteer organizing, community leadership, or field work- Experience managing people or volunteer teams- Excellent interpersonal, facilitation, and communication skills- Comfortable using Mobilize, Google Workspace, and volunteer tracking systems- Commitment to developing local leaders and working with diverse communities

Compensation & Progression

This position follows a phase-based compensation model:| Phase   | $ Raised     | Compensation & Role Evolution                                  ||---------|--------------|------------------------------------------------------------------|| Phase 0 | $0–$100K     | Volunteer – helps organize events, coordinate early outreach     || Phase 1 | $100K–$500K  | Paid hourly – builds volunteer lists, runs meetings, coordinates || Phase 2 | $500K–$1.25M | Full-time salaried – leads state-wide program and team building  || Phase 3+| $1.25M+      | Manages full leadership track, training systems, and reporting   |

Leadership Structure

The Volunteer Director reports to the Field Director and Campaign Manager. They collaborate with Operations, Communications, and Digital teams to drive turnout and create volunteer experiences that are meaningful, effective, and community-led.
