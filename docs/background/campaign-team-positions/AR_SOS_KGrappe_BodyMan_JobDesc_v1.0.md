Body Man – Kelly Grappe for Secretary of State

Campaign Philosophy & Culture

The Kelly Grappe for Secretary of State campaign is a mission-driven movement emphasizing trust, momentum, local leadership, and adaptability. Every team member operates as part of a cohesive, mission-focused team. We are not merely hiring for jobs; we are inviting individuals into a strategy built on shared values and collective goals.All team members initially wear multiple hats, with duties evolving as we achieve funding milestones and expand our personnel. The Body Man plays a pivotal role in this structure, requiring flexibility, resilience, and a commitment to the campaign's overarching mission.

Role Overview

The Body Man serves as the Candidate's closest aide, providing comprehensive logistical and personal support to ensure the Candidate's daily activities run smoothly. This role encompasses managing schedules, coordinating travel, preparing briefing materials, and addressing immediate needs, allowing the Candidate to focus on campaign objectives.

Key Responsibilities

- Schedule Management: Maintain and coordinate the Candidate's daily schedule, ensuring punctuality and preparedness for all engagements.- Travel Coordination: Arrange travel logistics, including transportation and accommodations, ensuring seamless transitions between events.- Briefing Preparation: Compile and organize briefing materials for meetings, events, and public appearances.- Personal Assistance: Address the Candidate's immediate needs, from meal arrangements to wardrobe coordination, ensuring optimal readiness.- Liaison Duties: Act as the primary point of contact between the Candidate and campaign staff, volunteers, and external stakeholders.- Confidentiality Maintenance: Handle sensitive information with utmost discretion and professionalism.

Qualifications

- Proven experience in executive support or personal assistant roles, preferably within political or high-pressure environments.- Exceptional organizational and multitasking abilities.- Strong interpersonal and communication skills.- Ability to anticipate needs and proactively address challenges.- Commitment to the campaign's mission and values.- Flexibility to travel and adapt to a dynamic schedule.

Leadership Structure

The Body Man will report directly to the Candidate and coordinate closely with the Strategic Director. As the campaign scales, this role evolves to encompass greater strategic responsibilities, including mentorship of junior staff and representation of the campaign in high-stakes environments.
