Communications Director – Kelly Grappe for Secretary of State

Campaign Philosophy & Culture

The Kelly Grappe for Secretary of State campaign is a mission-driven movement emphasizing trust, momentum, local leadership, and adaptability. Every team member operates as part of a cohesive, mission-focused team. We are not merely hiring for jobs; we are inviting individuals into a strategy built on shared values and collective goals.All team members initially wear multiple hats, with duties evolving as we achieve funding milestones and expand our personnel. The Communications Director plays a pivotal role in this structure, requiring flexibility, resilience, and a commitment to the campaign's overarching mission.

Role Overview

The Communications Director is responsible for developing and executing the campaign's messaging strategy, managing media relations, overseeing digital communications, and ensuring consistent and effective communication across all platforms. This role is critical in shaping the public narrative and enhancing the Candidate's visibility and reputation.

Key Responsibilities

- Develop and implement comprehensive messaging strategy across all platforms- Oversee daily press operations, including media advisories, releases, and interviews- Manage social media team, email outreach, and website content- Serve as spokesperson or prepare candidate surrogates for press engagements- Rapid response to breaking news and opposition messaging- Collaborate with Strategic Director and Field/Community teams to ensure messaging cohesion

Qualifications

- Proven experience in political communications, preferably in campaign settings- Strong background in media relations and crisis communication- Expertise in digital communications and social media strategy- Excellent writing and speaking skills- Ability to thrive under pressure and meet fast-moving deadlines

Compensation & Progression

This position follows a phase-based growth model:| Phase   | $ Raised     | Compensation & Role Evolution                                      ||---------|--------------|--------------------------------------------------------------------|| Phase 0 | $0–$100K     | Volunteer role supporting overall messaging tasks                 || Phase 1 | $100K–$500K  | Transition to paid role with full oversight of daily communications|| Phase 2 | $500K–$1.25M | Expansion to managing full comms team and digital/press strategy   || Phase 3+| $1.25M+      | Direct comprehensive public and internal communications strategy   |

Leadership Structure

The Communications Director will report to the Campaign Manager and work in close partnership with the Strategic Director. As the campaign scales, this role will lead the internal messaging team and serve as a core member of campaign leadership in all public-facing strategy.
