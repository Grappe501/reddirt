Campaign Timeline & Milestone-Based Personnel Plan

This document outlines the chronological fundraising milestones that trigger staff expansion, compensation increases, infrastructure growth, and leadership delegation. These benchmarks guide when and how the campaign can scale in line with budget.

Phase 0: Pre-Ramp (Volunteer Core)

Target: $0–$100,000

Timeframe: April–May 2025

Core Team (Volunteers Only): Chris, Steve, core directors onboarding in volunteer capacity

Personnel: 5–8 core planners on board

Focus: Launch listening tour, event planning, digital setup, messaging framework

Phase 1: Early Activation

Target: $100,000–$500,000

Timeframe: May–June 2025

Hire Begins: First 3 paid positions activated (Campaign Manager, Field Director, Body Man)

Personnel Count: Up to 5 paid

Events/Week: 1–2

System: Clock-in system goes live

Start hourly rate for early volunteers >10hrs/week

Phase 2: Expansion Begins

Target: $500,000–$1.25 million

Timeframe: June–July 2025

Add Roles: Communications Director, Operations Director, 1 Local Organizer, Finance Director

Personnel Count: 10–12 paid

Events/Week: 3–4

First regional office opens (treated as advertising unit)

All 40hr/week staff salaried at base rate

Phase 3: Full County-Level Ramp

Target: $1.25 million–$3 million

Timeframe: July–August 2025

Add Roles: Regional Field Organizers, Digital Director, Content Creator, Press Secretary

Personnel Count: 15–20 paid

Events/Week: 4–5

Offices: 4–6 active statewide

Precinct-level data input and engagement begins

Phase 4: Momentum Build / $5M Marker

Target: $3 million–$5 million

Timeframe: August–Early September 2025

Add Roles: Scheduling Coordinator, Compliance Manager, HR & Training, additional Local Organizers

Personnel Count: 25–30

Milestone Bonus: All hourly and salary rates increase 5–10%

Events/Week: 5–6

Phase 5: Major Lift / Media Phase

Target: $5 million–$10 million

Timeframe: Mid-September–Early October 2025

Leadership Finalized: Full inner director team fully salaried

Field Offices: 10+ activated

High-Touch Events: Daily events, candidate travel, increased ad buys

Clock-In Enforcement: Hours capped per role unless strategic approval

Bonus Pool Structure Built in for $10M attainment

Phase 6: Post-$10M Strategy

Target: $10 million and beyond

Timeframe: Final 30 days

Bonus Disbursement: Based on contribution, hours, tenure

New Funds Distribution: 20% of overage split among team

Scale-up Capacity: Funds for flex staff, mobile units, GOTV surge staffing

Comms/Field Pay Raise: +10–15% in final month for full-time staff
