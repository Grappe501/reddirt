Research & Policy Director – Kelly Grappe for Secretary of State

Campaign Philosophy & Culture

We believe facts matter. In an era of noise and disinformation, we lead with clarity, values, and truth. The Research & Policy Director ensures our policy positions, public statements, and contrast messages are grounded in knowledge, strategy, and community impact.

Role Overview

The Research & Policy Director is responsible for developing issue positions, conducting opposition research, building candidate briefing materials, and supporting rapid response to political developments. This role supports the Communications, Digital, and Field teams with accurate, timely, and impactful information.

Key Responsibilities

- Maintain database of research briefs, talking points, and policy documents- Draft position statements, debate prep guides, and issue one-pagers- Track opponent voting records, financial interests, and public statements- Coordinate with Communications and Digital teams on earned and paid messaging- Support rapid-response research needs and breaking news fact-checks- Prepare surrogate memos and help train candidate and team for issue-based Q&A

Qualifications

- 3+ years in political research, policy development, advocacy, or legislative work- Strong research, writing, editing, and messaging skills- Comfort with policy translation—turning complex issues into people-first language- Experience in opposition research or campaign strategy strongly preferred- Commitment to truth, rigor, and strategic communication

Compensation & Progression

This role follows a milestone-based growth model:| Phase   | $ Raised     | Compensation & Role Evolution                                       ||---------|--------------|----------------------------------------------------------------------|| Phase 0 | $0–$100K     | Volunteer – drafts issue briefs and builds research template         || Phase 1 | $100K–$500K  | Paid hourly – manages oppo tracking, policy docs, and Q&A guidance   || Phase 2 | $500K–$1.25M | Full-time salaried – leads research strategy and policy messaging     || Phase 3+| $1.25M+      | Manages research team, debate prep, and policy transition docs       |

Leadership Structure

The Research & Policy Director reports to the Communications Director and Strategic Director, and supports all departments with policy and message alignment. They are responsible for keeping the campaign grounded in fact, connected to local needs, and ready to respond to any narrative opportunity or threat.
