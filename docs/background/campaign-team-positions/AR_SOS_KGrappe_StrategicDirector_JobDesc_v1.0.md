Strategic Director – Kelly Grappe for Secretary of State

Campaign Philosophy & Culture

This campaign is a movement, not a machine. Every team member is expected to operate as part of a mission-focused team. We aren’t hiring for jobs—we’re inviting you into a strategy built on trust, momentum, local leadership, and adaptability.All team members will wear multiple hats at the beginning. Duties will evolve as we hit funding milestones and bring on new personnel. The job you’re applying for may not resemble a traditional campaign job—and that’s by design.We’re building from scratch. And that means everyone is a builder first.

Role Overview

The Strategic Director is the chief architect of the campaign’s long-term vision and execution plan. This role begins as a volunteer position, encompassing the responsibilities of Body Man, Campaign Manager, and Field Director. As the campaign progresses and achieves fundraising milestones, the Strategic Director will transition into a full-time, salaried position, focusing exclusively on strategic oversight and leadership.

Campaign Progression Plan

| Phase | $ Raised | Primary Responsibilities for this Role ||-------|----------|-----------------------------------------|| Phase 0 | $0–$100K | Role fulfilled by volunteer lead (Steve or other); supports full team workload || Phase 1 | $100K–$500K | Takes over Body Man responsibilities; supports Field & CM roles || Phase 2 | $500K–$1.25M | Trained volunteer or new hire takes Body Man full-time; Strategic Director shifts to oversee geographic targeting, team development, message cohesion || Phase 3+ | $1.25M+ | Role becomes specialized and supported by direct reports or deputies |

Compensation Model

- Starts as volunteer role- Converts to hourly at 20% discount once >10hrs/week sustained- Becomes full salary at low range at 40hrs/week- Receives bump and bonus at 50hrs/week- Raises and bonuses applied as outlined in milestone plan

Job Duties by Phase

**Phase 0:**- Volunteer-level planning, coordination, reporting to Steve/Chris- Research, note-taking, administrative duties across all departments**Phase 1:**- Fulfill primary duties of this role (e.g., Body Man)- ALSO manage responsibilities of Field Director + assist Campaign Manager- Support event logistics, staff briefings, local outreach**Phase 2:**- Begin streamlining responsibilities- Train incoming staff or volunteers- Oversee day-to-day execution of this role while coordinating with team leads- Strategic input into field operations and geography**Phase 3+:**- Take full ownership of the position- Delegate to team- Report to senior leadership- Participate in strategic, cross-functional planning meetings

Ideal Background & Qualities

- Proven experience in campaign strategy and management- High emotional intelligence and team-first attitude- Adaptive, mission-aligned, transparent communicator- Committed to equity, leadership development, and civic transformation in Arkansas
