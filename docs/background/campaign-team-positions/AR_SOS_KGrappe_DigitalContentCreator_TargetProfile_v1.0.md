Internal Campaign Memo: Digital Content Creator – Kelly Grappe for Secretary of State

Campaign Philosophy & Culture

This campaign is storytelling at scale. The Digital Content Creator helps us meet people where they are—online—with compelling, consistent, and human-centered content. From Instagram reels to campaign graphics, this role turns our values into visuals and our strategy into shareable story.

Role Overview

The Digital Content Creator supports the Digital Director by producing videos, graphics, and written content for all online platforms. They generate engaging material that fuels social media, email, SMS, and website outreach. They are responsible for helping maintain a consistent visual brand, fast-turnaround content, and emotionally resonant creative work.

Ideal Candidate Profile

Enneagram Type: Type 4 (The Individualist) with Wing 3 (The Achiever)- Type 4 Traits: Creative, expressive, original- Wing 3 Traits: Deadline-driven, visual communicatorDISC Profile: High I (Influence) and High C (Conscientiousness)- High I Traits: Energetic, message-focused, socially aware- High C Traits: Attention to detail, visual quality controlThe ideal candidate is a creative self-starter who understands political tone, visual identity, and social media virality. They know how to find a moment, package it, and post it before the moment passes.

Preferred Experience

- Experience creating video, graphic, and written content across multiple platforms- Proficiency in Canva, Adobe Creative Suite, CapCut, or similar tools- Understanding of brand consistency, audience targeting, and digital storytelling- Bonus: political, advocacy, or nonprofit digital comms experience

Campaign Progression Plan

| Phase     | $ Raised       | Role Evolution                                                  ||-----------|----------------|-----------------------------------------------------------------|| Phase 0   | $0–$100K       | Volunteer – supports video, social templates, and graphics       || Phase 1   | $100K–$500K    | Paid hourly – produces daily content and manages visual library || Phase 2   | $500K–$1.25M   | Full-time salaried – leads brand consistency and rapid response || Phase 3+  | $1.25M+        | Manages team of editors/designers or contracts freelancers       |

Compensation Model

- Phase 0: Volunteer- Phase 1: Hourly (discounted early rate)- Phase 2: Full-time salaried (40 hrs/week)- Phase 3+: Bonus eligible, equipment stipend, or creative services support

Key Responsibilities by Phase

Phase 0:- Help design basic graphic templates and edit short video clips- Assist in building the content calendarPhase 1:- Shoot or edit video for candidate appearances- Create content for email, social, SMS, and paid- Track engagement and adjust creative style as neededPhase 2:- Maintain creative style guide and brand kit- Lead rapid-response visual storytelling for news or debate moments- Work with volunteers to gather user-generated contentPhase 3+:- Manage team of creators or contractors- Own video content from concept to delivery- Archive all visual assets and support GOTV creative deployment

Leadership Progression

The Digital Content Creator reports to the Digital Director and works closely with the Communications and Field teams. They help translate the campaign’s message into the everyday online experience of voters across Arkansas.
