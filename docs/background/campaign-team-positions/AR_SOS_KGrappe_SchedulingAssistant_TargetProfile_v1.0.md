Internal Campaign Memo: Scheduling Assistant – Kelly Grappe for Secretary of State

Campaign Philosophy & Culture

Scheduling is strategy. The Scheduling Assistant ensures that every meeting, event, and appearance is timely, intentional, and executed with care. They help make the most of the Candidate’s time while keeping the campaign running smoothly behind the scenes.

Role Overview

The Scheduling Assistant supports the Scheduling Coordinator by helping track requests, prep briefing materials, and ensure communication flows between departments. They are responsible for calendar upkeep, daily prep docs, and flagging conflicts across the schedule.

Ideal Candidate Profile

Enneagram Type: Type 6 (The Loyalist) with Wing 1 (The Reformer)- Type 6 Traits: Supportive, diligent, responsibility-driven- Wing 1 Traits: Organized, principled, task-focusedDISC Profile: High S (Steadiness) and High C (Conscientiousness)- High S Traits: Dependable, calm, adaptable- High C Traits: Detail-oriented, proactive, checklist-drivenThe ideal candidate is calm, focused, and reliable—someone who thrives supporting others and making sure everything runs like clockwork.

Preferred Experience

- Administrative or scheduling experience in a fast-paced environment- Strong communication and time management skills- Proficiency in Google Calendar and spreadsheet tools- Comfort interacting with stakeholders, staff, and volunteers- Prior campaign or event coordination experience a plus

Campaign Progression Plan

| Phase     | $ Raised       | Role Evolution                                           ||-----------|----------------|----------------------------------------------------------|| Phase 0   | $0–$100K       | Volunteer – assists with internal calendar tracking       || Phase 1   | $100K–$500K    | Paid hourly – manages prep docs and daily checklists      || Phase 2   | $500K–$1.25M   | Full-time salaried – coordinates Candidate day-to-day     || Phase 3+  | $1.25M+        | May manage public scheduling intake and briefing folders  |

Compensation Model

- Phase 0: Volunteer- Phase 1: Hourly at discounted 20% rate for >10hrs/week- Phase 2: Full-time salaried (40 hrs/week)- Phase 3+: Travel support stipend or assistant role expansion

Key Responsibilities by Phase

Phase 0:- Support meeting notes and calendar updates- Help manage request tracking spreadsheetPhase 1:- Prepare daily briefing documents for Candidate and campaign lead- Coordinate reminders, travel timing, and documentation- Assist with scheduling communications across teamsPhase 2:- Maintain master calendar in coordination with Scheduling Coordinator- Liaise with departments to collect scheduling needs- Troubleshoot conflicts and logistics gaps in the schedulePhase 3+:- Oversee full-day schedules and prep packets for events or meetings- Help manage multi-day tour schedules- Archive all scheduling documents and manage final calendar log

Leadership Progression

The Scheduling Assistant reports to the Scheduling Coordinator and Operations Director. This role is essential in keeping the Candidate and team organized, responsive, and running on time during the most intense phases of the campaign.
