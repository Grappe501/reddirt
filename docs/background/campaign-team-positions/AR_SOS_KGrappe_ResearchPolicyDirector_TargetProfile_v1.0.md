Internal Campaign Memo: Research & Policy Director – Kelly Grappe for Secretary of State

Campaign Philosophy & Culture

In a race built on values, vision, and facts, the Research & Policy Director is the architect of our message integrity. This role ensures that every public statement, debate response, and policy position is grounded in research, aligned with community needs, and strategically framed.

Role Overview

The Research & Policy Director leads campaign-wide policy development, opposition research, issue analysis, and rapid-response briefing. They manage internal and external policy documents, candidate briefing materials, legislative summaries, and work closely with Comms, Digital, and Field to shape message clarity. This person is the keeper of receipts, defender of facts, and translator of policy into plain language and political impact.

Ideal Candidate Profile

Enneagram Type: Type 5 (The Investigator) with Wing 6 (The Loyalist)- Type 5 Traits: Analytical, detail-driven, cerebral- Wing 6 Traits: Loyal, thorough, systems-orientedDISC Profile: High C (Conscientiousness) and High D (Dominance)- High C Traits: Fact-oriented, researcher, precise- High D Traits: Strategic, outcome-driven, disciplinedThe ideal candidate is someone who lives for the footnotes and shines behind the curtain. They are intellectually curious, obsessed with clarity, and wired for strategy. They provide the fuel for contrast messaging and the facts behind the narrative.

Preferred Experience

- 2+ cycles or 3+ years in issue advocacy, policy research, or legislative work- Strong familiarity with state and federal legislative databases, public records, and campaign research tools- Excellent writing, editing, and strategic framing skills- Ability to translate policy into community benefit or campaign contrast- Bonus: experience in opposition research and/or debate prep

The Research & Policy Director reports to the Communications Director and Strategic Director. They become a core part of the narrative development team—shaping how the campaign explains what we believe, how we’ll lead, and why voters can trust us to deliver.
