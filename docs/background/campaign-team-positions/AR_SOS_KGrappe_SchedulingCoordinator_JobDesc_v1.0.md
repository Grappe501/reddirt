Scheduling Coordinator – Kelly Grappe for Secretary of State

Campaign Philosophy & Culture

A people-powered campaign moves fast, and time is our most limited asset. The Scheduling Coordinator ensures every hour is used wisely by organizing the Candidate’s calendar and coordinating all internal and external scheduling. This position is essential to keeping the campaign focused, strategic, and well-aligned.

Role Overview

The Scheduling Coordinator manages the daily and weekly calendar of the Candidate and senior leadership. They coordinate internal meeting requests, travel logistics, public event scheduling, and ensure strategic alignment between departments. This role requires discretion, diplomacy, speed, and rock-solid organization.

Key Responsibilities

- Maintain and communicate the Candidate’s calendar daily- Intake and evaluate scheduling requests from all departments and external stakeholders- Coordinate logistics for campaign stops, meetings, and events- Ensure no conflicts or duplication in internal meetings and field appearances- Communicate scheduling changes to relevant departments in real-time- Provide prep documentation and time/location info in advance of all events

Qualifications

- 1–2 cycles or 2+ years in scheduling, executive support, or administrative coordination- Exceptional attention to detail and time management- Strong written and verbal communication skills- Comfort navigating multiple departments and stakeholder relationships- Proficiency with Google Calendar, spreadsheets, and communication platforms

Compensation & Progression

This role follows a milestone-based growth structure:| Phase   | $ Raised     | Compensation & Role Evolution                                  ||---------|--------------|------------------------------------------------------------------|| Phase 0 | $0–$100K     | Volunteer – helps intake requests and track basic availability   || Phase 1 | $100K–$500K  | Paid hourly – manages scheduling workflow and coordination       || Phase 2 | $500K–$1.25M | Full-time salaried – owns full calendar and logistics workflow   || Phase 3+| $1.25M+      | May supervise assistant support or manage advanced logistics     |

Leadership Structure

The Scheduling Coordinator reports to the Operations Director and collaborates closely with the Candidate’s Body Man, Campaign Manager, and Communications Director. This role ensures every meeting, campaign stop, and internal decision is executed smoothly and efficiently.
