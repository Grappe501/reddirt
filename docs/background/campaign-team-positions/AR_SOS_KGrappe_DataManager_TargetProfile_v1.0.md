Internal Campaign Memo: Data Manager – Kelly Grappe for Secretary of State

Campaign Philosophy & Culture

Every great field plan, message, and resource decision starts with data. The Data Manager is the engine behind targeting, tracking, and adjusting our campaign tactics in real-time. This role turns numbers into strategy and ensures every door knock, call, and dollar is used wisely.

Role Overview

The Data Manager oversees the campaign’s voter targeting, database management, performance tracking, and reporting. They manage VAN/NGP systems, build targeted universes for field and digital programs, and track progress to goals. They also support cross-department reporting for volunteer engagement, donor targeting, and event metrics.

Ideal Candidate Profile

Enneagram Type: Type 5 (The Investigator) with Wing 1 (The Reformer)- Type 5 Traits: Analytical, process-minded, independent thinker- Wing 1 Traits: Ethical, detail-focused, systems-awareDISC Profile: High C (Conscientiousness) and High D (Dominance)- High C Traits: Structured, data-oriented, rule-following- High D Traits: Results-driven, focused on optimization and improvementThe ideal candidate is someone who thrives behind the scenes, speaks spreadsheet fluently, and is always asking, 'How can we work smarter?' They are methodical, precise, and highly collaborative across departments.

Preferred Experience

- 2+ cycles or 3+ years in campaign data, analytics, or field operations- Proficiency in VAN/NGP, Excel/Sheets, and preferably GIS or SQL tools- Experience with voter universe creation, reporting dashboards, and goal tracking- Understanding of relational data, list segmentation, and API integrations- Comfort training others on data best practices

Campaign Progression Plan

| Phase     | $ Raised       | Role Evolution                                                ||-----------|----------------|----------------------------------------------------------------|| Phase 0   | $0–$100K       | Volunteer – cleans data, supports list building, reporting     || Phase 1   | $100K–$500K    | Paid hourly – manages VAN, field/digital targeting, reporting  || Phase 2   | $500K–$1.25M   | Full-time salaried – builds full data pipeline and dashboards  || Phase 3+  | $1.25M+        | Manages data team, voter modeling, and analytics integration   |

Compensation Model

- Phase 0: Volunteer- Phase 1: Hourly at discounted 20% rate for >10hrs/week- Phase 2: Full-time salaried (40 hrs/week)- Phase 3+: Eligible for bonus pool and may supervise analyst or visualization support

Key Responsibilities by Phase

Phase 0:- Organize VAN data structures and cleanup tasks- Set up basic field metrics and volunteer trackingPhase 1:- Create walk lists, phone banks, and digital targeting lists- Coordinate with Field, Digital, and Comms for target alignment- Begin building real-time tracking dashboardsPhase 2:- Manage voter contact data entry and analytics- Run and evaluate persuasion, turnout, and support universes- Produce weekly performance and accountability reportsPhase 3+:- Integrate modeling, polling, and targeting overlays- Manage multi-layered dashboards across departments- Lead precinct, ward, and county- and statewide targeting refinement

Leadership Progression

The Data Manager reports to the Strategic Director and collaborates closely with Field, Digital, Finance, and Communications. By late campaign, they are a key voice in targeting, turnout strategy, and data-driven evaluation of all campaign activity.
