Internal Campaign Memo: Compliance Manager – Kelly Grappe for Secretary of State

Campaign Philosophy & Culture

The Kelly Grappe for Secretary of State campaign is a people-powered operation built on transparency, trust, and strategic growth. We believe that strong systems are the foundation for bold action—and nowhere is that more important than in compliance. The Compliance Manager protects the campaign’s integrity, ensures legal accuracy, and empowers the finance team to work with confidence and clarity.

Role Overview

The Compliance Manager is responsible for maintaining accurate financial records, filing timely required campaign finance reports with the appropriate Arkansas authorities, managing disbursements and reimbursements, tracking contributions, and ensuring full compliance with campaign finance laws. This person will work closely with the Finance Director and Treasurer, and eventually oversee vendor contract tracking, audit readiness, and contribution limits.
