Volunteer Onboarding Packet – Kelly Grappe for Secretary of State

Thank you for joining our people-powered movement! This campaign is driven by Arkansans who believe in community, compassion, and bold leadership. As a volunteer, you are the face and voice of our campaign. Whether you’re making calls, knocking on doors, creating content, or helping organize your precinct—your time and talent matter. This packet will walk you through expectations, how to get started, and who to contact if you have questions.

Our Values

• Community first – We lead with listening and serve every neighborhood.

• Courage with compassion – We speak truth with love, even when it’s hard.

• People over politics – Our movement centers real people, not party bosses.

• Faith in action – We believe in justice, equity, and the dignity of work.

Volunteer Expectations

• Represent the campaign with professionalism and kindness.

• Show up on time, prepared, and ready to support your team.

• Ask questions if you're unsure—there's no such thing as a silly question!

• Respect all people, regardless of political beliefs or background.

• Track your work accurately (canvass sheets, calls, data entry, etc.).

Getting Started

• Attend a Volunteer Orientation (check email or ask your lead).

• Join our Slack or text group for regular updates.

• Sign up for at least one shift per week—more if you can!

• Review the training materials specific to your role.

• Reach out with ideas, suggestions, or feedback—we're a team!

Who to Contact

• General Questions: volunteer@kellygrappe.com

• Field & Canvassing: field@kellygrappe.com

• Phone Banking & Recruitment: phones@kellygrappe.com

• Events & Logistics: events@kellygrappe.com

• Social Media Team: digital@kellygrappe.com

Next Steps

• Add our campaign email to your safe sender list.

• Bookmark our campaign calendar and sign up for events.

• Follow us on social media and share posts regularly.

• Invite 3 friends to volunteer with you!

• Celebrate your impact—you are what wins this campaign!
