Internal Campaign Memo: Operations Associate – Kelly Grappe for Secretary of State

Campaign Philosophy & Culture

No campaign wins without people who make sure everything works behind the scenes. The Operations Associate keeps our campaign moving—literally. From delivering yard signs to prepping event kits to running checklists before big campaign stops, this person makes the plan real.

Role Overview

The Operations Associate is responsible for logistics execution across campaign departments. They assist with supply runs, print production, delivery logistics, field office support, and internal communications systems. They work closely with the Operations Director and Event, Field, and Volunteer teams.

They’re not flashy—but they’re foundational. They care about the details and keep things organized under pressure.

Preferred Experience

- Prior experience in political organizing, logistics, or operations preferred- Reliable transportation and ability to lift campaign supplies as needed- Proficiency in Google Workspace, spreadsheets, and checklists- Ability to problem-solve and adapt when plans shift last minute
