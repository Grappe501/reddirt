Operations Director – Kelly Grappe for Secretary of State

Campaign Philosophy & Culture

The Kelly Grappe for Secretary of State campaign is a dynamic, grassroots-powered movement grounded in strategic execution, equity, and operational excellence. Every campaign team member must be nimble, collaborative, and committed to serving Arkansas communities with integrity and innovation. The Operations Director ensures that vision becomes action by managing campaign systems, logistics, and internal operations at every phase of growth.

Role Overview

The Operations Director is responsible for managing day-to-day internal systems, scheduling, logistics, HR coordination, and field infrastructure. This position is central to organizing and executing campaign events, building internal workflows, managing vendors and supplies, and ensuring smooth communication across departments. This role requires strategic foresight, calm under pressure, and excellent logistical instincts.

Key Responsibilities

- Maintain and oversee master campaign calendar, scheduling candidate and staff travel and events- Manage field office and event logistics, including space booking, transportation, and supply inventory- Oversee onboarding and offboarding processes for staff and volunteers- Maintain vendor relationships and coordinate delivery of materials and tech- Organize systems for internal communication, project management, and documentation- Coordinate logistics for campaign launches, town halls, and GOTV operations

Qualifications

- Experience in operations, logistics, or project management (2+ years preferred)- Familiarity with political campaigns or mission-driven organizations- Excellent organizational and time-management skills- Knowledge of tools like Google Workspace, project trackers (e.g., Trello, Asana), Mobilize, VAN- Comfort with tight deadlines, high volume, and shifting priorities

Compensation & Progression

This position follows a milestone-based growth model:| Phase   | $ Raised     | Compensation & Role Evolution                                  ||---------|--------------|------------------------------------------------------------------|| Phase 0 | $0–$100K     | Volunteer – calendar, basic scheduling, setup support            || Phase 1 | $100K–$500K  | Paid hourly – event logistics, onboarding, vendor coordination   || Phase 2 | $500K–$1.25M | Full-time salaried – systems manager, leads ops communication    || Phase 3+| $1.25M+      | Team lead – oversees department of logistics, IT, HR, and tools  |

Leadership Structure

The Operations Director reports to the Campaign Manager and Strategic Director. This role works closely with the Field Director, Communications Director, and Finance Director to ensure all departments are supported operationally. The Operations Director is also responsible for training, onboarding, and internal documentation processes.
