**Status:** Internal research + safe metaphor guidance.  
**Last updated:** 2026-04-26 (Day 2).

---

## 1. Legal / factual frame (read first)

| Fact | Notes |
|------|--------|
| The **Arkansas Secretary of State** is **not** the **State Librarian** and **not** the **Arkansas State Library**. | The **State Library** is a **separate** agency; historically within **Department of Education** context—see [Arkansas State Library](https://www.library.arkansas.gov/) “About us.” |
| The SOS **is** a **constitutional office** with **broad public-facing record and elections responsibilities**. | Use **metaphor** language: *like* a civic **librarian** / **keeper of the record** / **shelves of democracy** (poetic, not a statutory title). |
| The official **Elections Division** page states it maintains the **state’s election records**; BCS records **business**, **notary**, **UCC**, **trademarks**, **apostille**, **authentications**; C&E notes **exhibits**, **civic education**, and **archives architectural drawings and other historical documents** in division description. | Single source: [Duties of the Office (Arkansas SOS)](https://sos.arkansas.gov/about-the-office/duties-of-the-office/) |

**Never say:** “The Secretary of State is Arkansas’s state librarian” as a **literal** title.  
**OK to say (metaphor / informal):** “A careful hand on the public record—**almost** like a civic librarian for the parts of our democracy you can’t hold in your hand.”

## 2. Official duties to cite when explaining the office (summary)

From **Duties of the Office** (SOS, accessed Day 2):

- **Business & Commercial Services (BCS):** name availability, entity filings, **trademarks**, **notary** certifications, **apostille/authentication of documents**, **UCC** filings. Physical locations: Victory Building (Little Rock), Northwest office (Fayetteville) — *verify phone/email from live page when publishing phone numbers*.

- **Communications & Education:** **elections**, Capitol, **Arkansas history**, **citizenship** education; **Young Voter Workshops**; **teacher** workshops; **exhibits** at the Capitol; **archives** of Capitol **architectural drawings** and other **historical documents**; **voter outreach** campaigns, etc.

- **Elections Division:** **“maintains the state’s election records”**; assists **county officials** with **federal, state, and district** elections; **NVRA** / **HAVA**-related roles in **statewide voter registration** system; **electronic voting** system training oversight; **clearinghouse** for **campaign finance reports** filed with **Arkansas Ethics Commission**; **The library of the Elections Division** maintains **journals and acts of the Legislature** and **incorporations and annexations** of cities and towns.

- **Capitol building / facilities / Capitol Police** — relevant to “capitol public face” and security; usually **not** central to “librarian” metaphor—use only when discussing **access** or **tours**.

- **State Capitol Gift Shop** — optional light touch only (service culture).

**Contrast:** [Arkansas State Library](https://www.library.arkansas.gov/) — **public library development**, interlibrary loan, resources for public libraries, etc.

## 3. Metaphorical language (approved directions)

| Phrase / idea | Use |
|---------------|-----|
| “Keeper of the public record” | Strong; **accurate in spirit** if tied to BCS + elections + legislative journals role. |
| “Like **Arkansas’s civic** librarian (for democracy’s paper trail)” | OK with **civic** / **for elections and filings** — **qualifier** in long copy. |
| “The **office** that helps Arkansans find **what’s official** and **what’s on the ballot**” | Strong SOS-specific. |
| “Keep the **books** straight—literally and figuratively” | Light; not accounting advice; good for business-audience. |
| “The **shelves** of democracy (filings, lists, **verified** petitions)” | Poetic; pair with one factual clause. |
| “The person voters trust to **get the list right** (candidates, measures, **deadlines**)” | **Process**-oriented. |

**Phrases to avoid (accuracy):**

- “**The** state librarian” (false title for SOS).  
- Implying the SOS **writes** all election law; they **administer** under law.  
- “Controls what books school libraries buy” (wrong agency lane).
