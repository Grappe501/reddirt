# Volunteer leveling system — progression model (GAME-1) (RedDirt)

**Packet GAME-1 (Part B).** Defines **levels** as **organizer maturity stages**: what people **do**, what **unlocks**, and how they **advance**. **Concept only**—no UI or migrations in this packet.

## 1. Design rules

- **Levels are not seats.** Holding a **county lead** position (ROLE-1) is a **staffing** decision; level reflects **earned organizer capacity** that may **qualify** someone to be considered for that seat (TALENT-1).
- **Advancement** uses **XP thresholds + qualitative gates**: e.g. Core 5 coverage, confirmed conversations, recruitment proof—see XP doc for numeric bands (illustrative until ops calibrates).
- **Regression** is **not** punitive by default; **momentum** may pause unlock offers if organizing evidence stalls, without stripping past achievements.

## 2. Levels

### 2.1 New Volunteer

| Dimension | Definition |
|-----------|------------|
| **What they do** | Onboards; learns tools; adds first relationships or completes first assignments under guidance; attends orientation or first event. |
| **What they unlock** | **Starter** message templates and scripts; **basic** training modules; **limited** assignment queue visibility (their own tasks); **no** assignment or POD leadership. |
| **How they progress** | Complete onboarding commitments; first **documented** outreach or relationship adds (post–REL-2); baseline XP threshold; optional POD leader **endorsement** for faster path. |

### 2.2 Organizer

| Dimension | Definition |
|-----------|------------|
| **What they do** | Sustains **regular** outreach; confirms registration status where possible; logs conversations honestly; meets **minimum** REL-1 KPI cadence for their role. |
| **What they unlock** | **Expanded** template/script library; **standard** training paths; higher **daily/weekly contact logging** caps (anti-spam still enforced); visibility into **“my network”** summary when REL-2 exists. |
| **How they progress** | Cumulative XP from **confirmed conversations**, **matches**, and **commitments**; **Core 5** progress toward completion; clean **data hygiene** (no duplicate gaming flags). |

### 2.3 Network Builder

| Dimension | Definition |
|-----------|------------|
| **What they do** | Maintains a **healthy** personal network beyond minimal KPIs; identifies **unregistered** voters and helps them toward registration; follows up across multiple touchpoints. |
| **What they unlock** | **Advanced** scripts (still human-sent); optional **county-scoped** insights for **their** contacts only; eligibility for **peer mentoring** badges; **invitation** to POD Core as member. |
| **How they progress** | XP from **unregistered identified + helped**, **multi-touch** sequences, **recruitment** of other volunteers into the program; evidence of **reciprocal** relationships (not cold list growth alone). |

### 2.4 Team Leader

| Dimension | Definition |
|-----------|------------|
| **What they do** | Coordinates a **small team** (e.g. POD slice or event team): assigns micro-tasks, checks in, rolls up blockers to county/field leads. |
| **What they unlock** | **Assign work** within scoped group (product + policy); **shared** team view of open items (future); **team** coaching content; may **recommend** unlocks to staff for teammates (advisory). |
| **How they progress** | Leadership XP: **successful recruit** journeys, **task completion** by mentees, **event** leadership; human **confirmation** of leadership behavior (TALENT-1 alignment—no auto-promotion). |

### 2.5 County Contributor

| Dimension | Definition |
|-----------|------------|
| **What they do** | Owns a **meaningful** slice of county outcomes: ties network to **`County`** / **`FieldUnit`** goals; participates in county briefings; surfaces file/data issues responsibly. |
| **What they unlock** | **Deeper** county insights (aggregated, privacy-preserving); participation in **county goal** narratives on dashboards; eligibility for **county-scoped** message variants approved by comms. |
| **How they progress** | XP weighted toward **county-attributed** REL-1 rollups + FIELD engagement; sustained **FieldAssignment** or equivalent geographic accountability; staff **affirmation** for data access tier. |

### 2.6 Regional Leader

| Dimension | Definition |
|-----------|------------|
| **What they do** | Spans **multiple counties** or **coordinates** county leads; develops **playbooks**; recruits **Team Leaders**; partners with staff on **scaling** relational program. |
| **What they unlock** | **Lead a POD** formally (aligned with POD doc); **leadership dashboards** (future packet) at scoped rollup; **advanced** training authorship or localization; priority access to **AI coaching** tuned for leaders. |
| **How they progress** | High cumulative XP with **leadership** mix; proven **multi-county** outcomes; **must** align with **position/seat** decisions—regional **title** in VPE does not replace HR/volunteer staffing votes. |

---

## 3. Tiers (optional grouping)

For **reporting only**, levels **1–2** = *Building*, **3–4** = *Multiplying*, **5–6** = *Anchoring*—labels can change; tiers **do not** replace level unlock tables.
