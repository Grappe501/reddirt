# County clerk social toolkit concept

Source thread: `https://chatgpt.com/share/69ee414d-b7f4-83ea-ac69-963227d34c17`

Status: planning intelligence for Kelly Grappe SOS campaign system. This is not legal advice, not an official county communication, and not a public promise that every county can be required to use these materials. Treat as a future office support concept and a campaign conversation aid.

## Strategic purpose

Equip local officials instead of criticizing them. A Secretary of State service model can help county clerks and election officials communicate accurate, repeatable information without making every county build graphics and copy from scratch.

Core line: transparency should be the standard.

## Future office package

- PDF guide with plain-language copy blocks
- Editable county-name templates
- Quarterly template drops before election windows
- Public notice checklist for agendas, minutes, recordings, and follow-up records
- Crisis communication basics for rumor control and corrections
- Voter education FAQ sheets

## Template families

### Voter registration reminder

Graphic concept: county courthouse or neutral county image with a simple registration headline.

Caption skeleton:

```text
Registering to vote in [COUNTY NAME] is straightforward.

You can register by mail or in person at [OFFICE / LOCATION].

Deadline for the upcoming election: [DATE].

Questions? Contact [PHONE / EMAIL] or visit [OFFICIAL LINK].
```

### Early voting reminder

Graphic concept: calendar-style post with the early voting window highlighted.

Caption skeleton:

```text
Early voting in [COUNTY NAME] begins [DATE].

Locations: [LOCATION LIST]
Hours: [HOURS]

Check your registration and make a plan before you go: [OFFICIAL LINK].
```

### Election Day reminder

Graphic concept: date-forward post with official county information only.

Caption skeleton:

```text
Election Day is [DATE].

Polls are open from [TIME] to [TIME].

Confirm your polling location here: [VOTER VIEW / OFFICIAL LINK].

If you are in line when polls close, stay in line.
```

### Absentee information

Graphic concept: checklist layout.

Caption skeleton:

```text
Need absentee voting information for [COUNTY NAME]?

Request deadline: [DATE]
Return deadline: [DATE]
Official details: [LINK]
Questions: [PHONE / EMAIL]
```

### New resident reminder

Graphic concept: "New to [COUNTY NAME]?" with local but nonpartisan image.

Caption skeleton:

```text
New to [COUNTY NAME]? Welcome.

If you recently moved, check whether your voter registration needs to be updated.

Start here: [OFFICIAL LINK]
```

### Youth voter reminder

Graphic concept: graduation or first-ballot theme, without school logos unless permission is granted.

Caption skeleton:

```text
Turning 18 by Election Day?

You may be eligible to register before your first election.

Check the official rules and deadlines here: [OFFICIAL LINK].
```

### What the clerk's office does

Graphic concept: office-service explainer with one role per post.

Caption skeleton:

```text
What does the [COUNTY NAME] Clerk's Office do?

We help maintain voter registration records, preserve public records, and provide public service to county residents.

Learn more: [OFFICIAL LINK].
```

## Transparency companion

Future public-records and meeting communication templates should help residents find:

- Meeting agendas
- Minutes
- Video or audio recordings when available
- Public records request instructions
- Previously released public records when disclosure is allowed

Working principle: public information should be public by default, with privacy, security, and legal exemptions respected.

## Compliance and brand guardrails

- The campaign should not use the official state seal as campaign branding.
- `Regnat Populus` may be referenced as civic education and constitutional framing.
- County seals, school logos, and official marks require permission and should stay on official channels unless counsel approves.
- Every template needs final local review for dates, office names, polling places, deadlines, and links.
- Do not include partisan asks in official-office templates.
