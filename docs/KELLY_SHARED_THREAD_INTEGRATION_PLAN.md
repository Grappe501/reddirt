## Method

For each thread:

1. **Read and summarize** the core thought.
2. **Classify impact**:
   - `public-copy`: can be safely expressed on voter-facing pages.
   - `internal-strategy`: useful for message discipline, staff, or AI context.
   - `program-vision`: future office initiative, clearly jurisdiction-aware.
   - `research-needed`: needs sourcing, legal review, authority mapping, or cost estimates.
   - `no-build`: context only.
3. **Integrate one thought at a time** into the smallest correct surface.
4. **Record source URL and decision** here.
