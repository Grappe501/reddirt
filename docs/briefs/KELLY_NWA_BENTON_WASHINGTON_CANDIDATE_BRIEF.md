## 1) Executive roll-up (2–3 lines)
NWA is the **highest population / highest attention** block in the Third District and **anchors** business media, students, and county clerks’ offices in two **large** counties. **Winning** here = **credible, calm competence** (SOS = records + elections + business filings) + **strong** college/small-city networks **(Washington)** + **business-acceptable** good-government and **reliability** language **(Benton)**. **Do not** treat NWA as one turf: **Benton** skews more **corporate/retail growth**; **Washington** has **U of A**, **higher** **density** of **college** students and **service** workers. **This brief** ties **one** opposition **legislative** list (all **elections + ballot initiative**-linked bills in research), **per-county** **plays**, and **KPI** placeholders. **No** **public** use of the opponent’s **name** off this printout without Steve.

## 2) Benton County — **summary (2–3 lines)**
**Heart** of the NWA **growth** story: **Rogers, Bentonville, Bella Vista**, and **Benton/ Centerton** orbits — **Walmart/ supplier** and **logistics** **culture**, **higher** **median** income, **retail** **and** **health** **employers**. **Path** to **vote** = **reliability of public records and business services**, **county** **clerk** **partnership** visibility, and **festival/field** **visibility** (see movement calendar: **Bentonville**-area fairs). **KPIs** and **turnout** **targets** = **TBD** from **CRM + voter file** when wired.

**Deep dive — Benton**
- **Narrative:** “The office that keeps **registrations, filings, and election lists** **straight** is the office that **keeps** **Northwest** **competitive** for **employers and families**.”
- **Geography (priority order for time):** Bentonville / Rogers / Siloam / gateway events → **Bella Vista** → **Gentry**-connected **festival** **lane** (see `arkansas-festivals-2026` / guide for **Benton** **county** **slugs**).
- **Field:** align **VFD** and **EHC** **intel** to **Benton** **chapters** (see `tentativeExternalEvents` + UAEX); **tentative** until **host** confirms. **Benton (Saline Co.)** **Amplify** **event** in guide is **Saline** — **do not** confuse with **Benton County**.
- **KPIs (fill from ops):** volunteer signups (Benton) · event touches (Benton) · donor conversations (NWA) · paid/organic reach (geo Benton) — **targets TBD**; owners Field / Events / Finance / Comms.
- **Risks:** **Over-assume** “blue” NWA; **SOS** is **low information** **down** ballot — **clarity** and **reputation** of **impeccable** **process** > partisan **volume**.

**Fayetteville** (**U of A**), **Springdale** (**fastest** **growth** and **bilingual** **reach**), **Johnson/Farmington** **corridor** — **strongest** **natural** **base** for **transparency, ballot** **access,** and **civic** **education** (matches **SOS** **priorities** and **direct** **democracy** story). **Win** = **turnout** **max** among **under-40**, **higher** **ed**, **service** **sector**, and **county** **civic** orgs; **reinforce** **clerk/early** **vote** **comfort**; **earned** **media** in **A-V** **buckets**.

**Deep dive — Washington**
- **Narrative:** “I’ll run the **list** the way you **run a project** you’re **proud of**—**documented,** **auditable,** and **fair**.”
- **Geography (priority order):** Fayetteville **(campus, Dickson, library partners)** → Springdale (community **associations,** **festival** **path**) → **West** Fayetteville / **Tontitown** **window** (events) → **Prairie** **Grove** as **rural** **outreach** **wedge** when scheduled.

- **KPIs (fill from ops):** campus contacts · early-vote education sessions · listening-session follow-ups · NWA Substack opens — **targets TBD**; owners campus / field / comms.
- **Risks:** **Student** **turnout** if **not** on **Dorm** or **Greek** **plumbing**; **Springdale** may need **bilingual** **materials** (check **sos-public** / **comms** **rules**).
